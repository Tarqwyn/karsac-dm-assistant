# Erwing Smallfoot

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/erwing`

> Cobalt Soul halfling monk; carries a coded message for Serris; does not know what it says.

## Overview

Erwing Smallfoot is a Cobalt Soul halfling monk. His arrival in Törweg is provisional — if the player attends, he arrives in Scene 3 by a different vessel (a Cobalt Soul charter). If the player does not attend, the thread holds: Erwing is still travelling, the message is still in motion, and the beat is picked up in a future session.

He arrived with a satchel and the careful caution of someone who has just completed the long part of a long journey.

## Background

On the road to Bysaes Tyl, Erwing was given a message by a Cobalt monk who had been discovered. The monk asked him to find Cumbria, then find Serris. The monk gave him the message and told him to deliver it. Erwing does not know what it says. He does not know why it matters. He carried it anyway, because the monk asked him to.

*"The Cobalt monk. On the road to Bysaes Tyl. He gave me this. He said to find Cumbria. He said to find you. He said to give it to you."*

Serris takes the coded message and reads it that evening in Brynja's hall. Serris goes very quiet.

The decoded content: *"Get word to the Cleric. I am discovered, trust no one, continue the work. It is now more important than ever. They are close to discovery. We must not allow them to succeed."*

Serris does not know who the Cleric is. Not yet.

## DM-Only

The Cobalt monk who gave Erwing the message was part of Erik Mathr's network — discovered by Mathr's intelligence and now presumed captured or dead. The message is the thread connecting the network's ongoing work to Serris's presence. "The Cleric" is Erik Mathr. Erwing does not know this. Serris does not know this yet. The players may piece it together.

Whether Erwing becomes part of Erik's network is not resolved in available sources. He carries the message because a dying man asked him to. That is the full scope of his known intention.
