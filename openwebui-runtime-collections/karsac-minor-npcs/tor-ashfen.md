# Tor Ashfen

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/tor-ashfen`

> Captain of the Coldfront; came back from Halvash different; Vane's men took him.

## Overview

Tor Ashfen captained the Coldfront out of Törweg, with Brix as his First Mate, for twelve years. Three weeks before the Chapter 2 events, he came back from a voyage to Halvash asking different questions, interested in different things — *"gentler than he had been before. That was what frightened me most"* (Brix). Two weeks ago, Vane's men came for him at the south dock warehouse. One man Brix recognised. The other had a black Ashvein pin. Ashfen walked with them. No struggle.

## DM-Only

Ashfen has been worked over by Vishara's methodology at the individual scale — the same process the Truthspeaker represents at a more advanced stage. He is alive, held in a small room in Törweg. He will not be in any condition to help the players in this session. His thread resumes in Valweg or later.

The visit to Halvash is what changed him. He saw something there — or asked the wrong question of the wrong person — and came back different. What he saw or asked is not specified in available sources.
