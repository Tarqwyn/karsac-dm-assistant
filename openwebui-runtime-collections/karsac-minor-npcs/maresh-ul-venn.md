> Senior Archivist of the Great Library of Vushhir; the methodological voice of the Sourcebook preface.

Maresh ul-Venn is Senior Archivist at the Great Library. His opening lecture to the Fourth Cartographic Faculty is the Sourcebook's preface epigraph: *"The first error of the young scholar is to mistake silence for absence. The second is to mistake testimony for truth. The third, and usually the most expensive, is to assume that any people has been waiting patiently to be understood by Vushhir."* He represents the Vushhiri faculty's self-aware methodological voice.
