# Elder Maren

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/elder-maren`
**Retrieval Summary:** Vushhiri scholar holding the minority etymology view that 'Lösweg' descends from a root meaning 'the wound that does not close.'

> Vushhiri scholar; holds the minority etymology view on the meaning of "Lösweg."

Elder Maren of the Great Library faculty holds the minority scholarly position that the name *Lösweg* descends from an older root meaning "the wound that does not close" — recorded alongside the more plausible derivations ("the land that endures" / "the place of waiting") without adjudication. Elder Maren's view is preserved in the Sourcebook as a scholarly minority position.
