# Hjalmar

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/hjalmar`
**Retrieval Summary:** The Carver's brother; went into the Maw three months ago; came out wrong; his fate is the Threshold-Bound Echo's testimony.

> The Carver's brother; went into the Maw three months ago; came out wrong; became whatever the Threshold-Bound Echo is.

## Overview

Hjalmar is the Carver's brother. Three months ago — around the time the Maw fracture opened — the two of them went up to the Stormwatch foothills. The Carver waited at the slot. Hjalmar went in. He came out looking the same, walking the same. He did not come out the same. The Carver cannot finish the sentence that describes what was different.

## DM-Only

The relationship between Hjalmar and the Threshold-Bound Echo is the Maw's deepest mystery and is deliberately left unspecified in sources. Possible readings: Hjalmar became the Echo; Hjalmar was consumed by whatever the Echo is and the Echo occupied the space he left; Hjalmar encountered something in the fracture that fundamentally altered his consciousness to the point where the Carver cannot recognise him as the same person.

The Carver's compulsive spiral-carving is the surface expression of Hjalmar's transformation propagating through someone who was present at the threshold of it.

The Threshold-Bound Echo is the entity the players encounter in the fracture. Whether it is or contains Hjalmar is not confirmed.

## Ambiguities

- The mechanism of Hjalmar's transformation is not specified in sources — the canon preserves this as an open question.
- Whether the Echo is Hjalmar is not confirmed.
