# The Skalds

**Canon File ID:** `factions/skalds`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/skalds`

**Entity Type:** Faction — tradition / guild

**Retrieval Summary:** The Skalds are the warrior-mystic tradition of Lösweg — keepers of oral history, genealogy, theology, law, and the ability to speak with the dead. Their silencing is one of Vishara's clearest symptoms: when Skalds stop speaking in Törweg, something is wrong. The Truthspeaker is a Skald whose tradition has been thinned. Ragnfrid is a practising Skald and the PC most attuned to tradition-level anomalies.

**Use For:** Identifying the Skalds as the Lösweg tradition institution and Ragnfrid's factional context, routing to the Skald Tradition concept.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/skalds`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Skald Tradition (the concept; the underlying practice; distinct from the institution), The Truthspeaker (a thinned Skald; not the institution).

**Key Relationships:**
- Ragnfrid — a practising Skald; the PC most sensitive to tradition damage.
- The Truthspeaker — a Skald whose tradition's weight is gone.
- Vishara — its thinning of the Skalds is a major campaign symptom.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe.
