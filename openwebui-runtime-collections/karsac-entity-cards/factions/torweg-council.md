# Törweg Council

**Canon File ID:** `factions/torweg-council`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/torweg-council`

**Entity Type:** Faction — governing body

**Retrieval Summary:** The Törweg Council is the advisory body formally subordinate to the Housecarl-Captain (currently Aldric Vane). In normal conditions it holds little power. In Chapter 2 it carries unusual weight because Vane has been distracted for two years executing Mathr's orders without question, leaving the council — and specifically Brynja — holding the city.

**Use For:** Identifying the Törweg Council as the city's governing body and Brynja's seat of operational authority, distinguishing it from the Lösweg Inner Council at Valweg.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/torweg-council`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Lösweg Inner Council (the Valweg-level council; Mathr and Beorn sit on it; distinct and senior to the Törweg Council).

**Key Relationships:**
- Brynja Thorgrimsdotter — has held the council's weight for two years.
- Aldric Vane — the Housecarl-Captain nominally above it.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed.
