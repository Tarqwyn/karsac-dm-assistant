# The Great Library of Vushhir

**Canon File ID:** `factions/great-library`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/great-library`

**Entity Type:** Faction — scholarly institution

**Retrieval Summary:** The Great Library of Vushhir is the civic-scholarly institution at the heart of Vushhir — simultaneously archive, university, observatory, court of debate, magical college, and diplomatic forum. It is the in-world institutional voice of the Sourcebook's first three parts. Serris trained there before her Cobalt Soul work. Senior Archivist Maresh ul-Venn and Archivist Solenne vel Dravik are among its named scholars.

**Use For:** Identifying the Great Library as the Vushhir scholarly institution, distinguishing it from the Cobalt Soul.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/great-library`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Cobalt Soul (the church-of-Ioun archive order; distinct institution with a different mission and location).

**Key Relationships:**
- Maresh ul-Venn — Senior Archivist.
- Archivist Solenne vel Dravik — Lösweg specialist.
- Serris — trained here before joining the Cobalt Soul.
