# Lösweg Inner Council

**Canon File ID:** `factions/losweg-council-inner`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/losweg-council-inner`

**Entity Type:** Faction — governing body

**Retrieval Summary:** The Lösweg Inner Council sits at Valweg. Jarl Beorn leads it. Jarl Mathr sits on it and has managed every detail of King Dugweb's annual visit for sixty years. The council is collectively deceived, not corrupted — Beorn is not shaped. Reaching Beorn before the next Dugweb visit is the Chapter 2–3 political objective.

**Use For:** Identifying the Lösweg Inner Council as the Valweg-level government, distinguishing it from the Törweg Council, and routing to the Chapter 3 political arc.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/losweg-council-inner`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Törweg Council (the city-level advisory body in Törweg; subordinate and distinct).

**Key Relationships:**
- Jarl Beorn — leads it; deceived not corrupted; the route to Dugweb.
- Jarl Mathr — sits on it; has shaped its relationship to the world for sixty years.
- King Dugweb — visits annually; Mathr has managed every visit.
