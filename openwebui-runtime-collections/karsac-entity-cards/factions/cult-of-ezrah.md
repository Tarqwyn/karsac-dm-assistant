# Cult of Ezrah

**Canon File ID:** `factions/cult-of-ezrah`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/cult-of-ezrah`

**Entity Type:** Faction — resistance network (DM-only)

**Retrieval Summary:** The Cult of Ezrah is Erik Mathr's resistance network — clerics of the god of liberation and terror working inside Vishara's political cage from within. Their methodology is patience and naming: identifying who can be reached, keeping threads alive, creating conditions for when someone arrives from outside. Erik is the network's operative inside House Mathr. The coded message's "the Cleric" refers to him.

**Use For:** Identifying the Cult of Ezrah as the resistance faction and Erik Mathr's network.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/cult-of-ezrah`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Ezrah the god (the divine force; the Cult serves it but they are distinct), House Mathr (the Vishara-vehicle Erik is infiltrating; distinct faction).

**Key Relationships:**
- Erik Mathr — the operative.
- Ezrah — the god they serve.
- Pell Duvash — a node Erik identified and reached.
