# The Cobalt Soul

**Canon File ID:** `factions/cobalt-soul`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/cobalt-soul`

**Entity Type:** Faction — religious order / archive

**Retrieval Summary:** The Cobalt Soul is an order within the church of Ioun dedicated to the preservation and distribution of truth. They maintain an archive at Halvash — the controlled point of Exandrian contact with Karsac. The Shadow-Walkers attacked the Halvash archive to recover the Pryzi key. Serris is the Cobalt Soul's operative travelling with the company.

**Use For:** Identifying the Cobalt Soul as the Halvash archive institution and Serris's faction, distinguishing it from the Great Library.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/cobalt-soul`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Great Library of Vushhir (a different scholarly institution; secular; distinct), Ioun (the god the Cobalt Soul serves; distinct from the order).

**Key Relationships:**
- Serris — their operative in the campaign.
- Halvash — their archive location; where the alley fight occurred.
- The Pryzi Key — taken from their archive.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe.
