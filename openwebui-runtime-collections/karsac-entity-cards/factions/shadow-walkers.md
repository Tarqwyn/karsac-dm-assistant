# The Shadow-Walkers

**Canon File ID:** `factions/shadow-walkers`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/factions/shadow-walkers`

**Entity Type:** Faction — operative network

**Retrieval Summary:** The Shadow-Walkers are Vishara's operational asset class in the field — not an organisation in the conventional sense but a class of operatives tracing their deepest roots to the proto-Yngondi cultures. They carry Ashvein equipment (throwing spikes, the same metal as the Truthspeaker's pin). Three attacked the company in the Halvash alley; one was killed; two escaped with the Pryzi key. The bone disc was recovered from the dead one.

**Use For:** Identifying the Shadow-Walkers as the Halvash alley attackers and Vishara's field operatives, routing to the Ashvein connection and Korvann's rune thread.

**Primary Detail File:** `factions/shadow-walkers`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Captains (a different Vishara/Maharuq-related force; not the Shadow-Walkers), House Mathr (the political vehicle; not the field operatives).

**Key Relationships:**
- Vishara — the force they serve.
- Ashvein — the equipment that identifies them; same metal as the Truthspeaker's pin.
- The Bone Disc — recovered from the one killed in Halvash.
- Korvann — the rune they carry connects to the rune Korvann has followed.
