# The Skald Tradition

**Canon File ID:** `concepts/skald-tradition`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/concepts/skald-tradition`

**Entity Type:** Concept

**Retrieval Summary:** The Skald Tradition is the structural fact of Lösweg culture — older than settlements. A fully trained Skald carries oral tradition entire: history, genealogy, theology, law, the long mythological cycles. They speak with the dead. To name something correctly is to participate in its existence. The Truthspeaker is a Skald whose tradition has been thinned. Ragnfrid is a practising Skald and perceives the Truthspeaker's thinning from outside.

**Use For:** Identifying the Skald Tradition as the Lösweg cultural framework, routing to Ragnfrid's class context and the Truthspeaker's thinning, distinguishing it from the Skalds faction.

**Primary Detail File:** `concepts/skald-tradition`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Skalds (the faction/guild of Skalds; the institution; distinct from the tradition concept), Ragnfrid (a practising Skald PC; the tradition as it applies to one character), The Truthspeaker (a Skald whose tradition has been thinned; not the tradition itself).

**Key Relationships:**
- Ragnfrid — the PC who embodies the tradition in the company.
- The Truthspeaker — a Skald whose tradition's weight has been eroded.
- Vishara — actively thins the Skald tradition as part of the Thinning of the Particular.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe.
