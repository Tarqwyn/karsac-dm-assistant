# Ashvein

**Canon File ID:** `items/ashvein`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/ashvein`

**Entity Type:** Item — material / connection marker

**Retrieval Summary:** Ashvein is a rare pre-Calamity glass-metal mined only beneath Halvash — neither glass nor metal, dark, with a specific quality. Three separate objects are made of it: the Shadow-Walkers' throwing spikes (Halvash alley), the Truthspeaker's small black pin (south dock), and Xyrrathh's dark vessel. The material is the supply-chain connection between the Shadow-Walker network and Mathr's council apparatus.

**Use For:** Identifying Ashvein as the material that connects the Shadow-Walkers to the Truthspeaker, routing to the supply-chain thread.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/ashvein`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Dark Vessel (Xyrrathh's item made of Ashvein; a specific object, not the material itself), The Bone Disc (Vishara's symbol; not Ashvein).

**Key Relationships:**
- Shadow-Walkers — their throwing spikes are Ashvein.
- The Truthspeaker — his pin is Ashvein; the connection to the Shadow-Walker supply chain.
- Xyrrathh — the dark vessel is Ashvein; an older acquisition.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The supply-chain implication is DM-layer; the material's presence is player-observable.
