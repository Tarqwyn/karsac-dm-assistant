# The Maw Carving (Extended Bone Disc Pattern)

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-maw-carving`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-maw-carving`

**Entity Type:** Item — handout (H13)

**Retrieval Summary:** The Maw Carving is the pattern scored on the Maw shaft walls — the same geometry as the bone disc, continued past where the disc's carving ends. Handout H13. Serris recognises it; goes quiet. Posted when a PC picks up the Aeorian note inside the Maw or when Serris recognises the wall carving.

**Use For:** Identifying the Maw Carving as a specific handout item and the wall-pattern discovery, distinguishing it from the place (the Maw) and the bone disc.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-maw-carving`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Maw (the physical location; the carving is on its walls, but the carving is the handout item), The Bone Disc (the object whose geometry the carving continues; distinct items), The Spiral Carver's Whittlings (the Cooperage NPC's output; same geometry, different object and location).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Bone Disc — the carving continues its geometry; the Maw is the extended pattern.
- Serris — recognises the connection and goes quiet.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe once discovered in Thread B.
