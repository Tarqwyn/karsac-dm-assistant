# The Scroll Case

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-scroll-case`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-scroll-case`

**Entity Type:** Item — artefact (key and map)

**Retrieval Summary:** The scroll case was found in Salsvault priced at fifteen gold among ordinary stock in Session 1. It is both key and map: the case itself opens the binding, and the map inside leads to the binding's location. The margin contains handwriting — the same hand as the Aeorian note inside the Maw, after two thousand years. Serris recognises this connection and goes quiet.

**Use For:** Identifying the scroll case as the Campaign 1 central artefact, routing to the binding and the same-hand connection.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-scroll-case`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Twelve Artefacts (the binding components; the scroll case is the key to the binding, not one of the twelve), The Aeorian Maw Fragment (the note with the same handwriting; a distinct object found in the Maw).

**Key Relationships:**
- Salsvault — where it was found.
- The Aeorian Maw Fragment — same hand; the connection Serris recognises.
- The Binding — the scroll case is the key to opening it.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The scroll case is player-held; its full function is DM-layer.
