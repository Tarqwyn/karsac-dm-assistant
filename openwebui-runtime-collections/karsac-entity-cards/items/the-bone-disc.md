# The Bone Disc

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-bone-disc`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-bone-disc`

**Entity Type:** Item — relic / Vishara symbol

**Retrieval Summary:** The bone disc is Vishara's oldest symbol, recovered from the Shadow-Walker killed in the Halvash alley. It has been warm since recovery. Floki saw it and said one word: "impossible." When Korvann is near it, there is a persistent sensation in the rune they have been following for ten years. The Maw walls are scored with its geometry, continued past where the disc ends.

**Use For:** Identifying the bone disc as the party's key artefact-clue carried since Session 1, distinguishing it from the twelve Stormwatch artefacts, and routing to the Vishara geometry thread.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-bone-disc`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Twelve Artefacts (the Stormwatch binding set; the bone disc is not one of them), The Pierced River-Stone (Floki's seventh artefact; distinct), The Maw Carving (the wall pattern that continues the bone disc's geometry; a handout, not the disc itself), The Spiral Carver's Whittlings (the Carver's output; same geometry, different object).

**Key Relationships:**
- Shadow-Walkers — it was on the body of the one killed in Halvash.
- Korvann — the rune they have followed for ten years resonates with the disc.
- The Maw — the walls continue the disc's geometry.
- Floki — said "impossible" when he saw it.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The full cosmological significance is DM-layer.
