# Mathr's Token

**Canon File ID:** `items/mathr-token`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/mathr-token`

**Entity Type:** Item — token / handout

**Retrieval Summary:** A flat bronze token, palm-sized, pierced once for a leather thong. Worn at the collar by Vane's housecarls at the south dock. The sigil on it is Mathr's house mark — not the Vane mark visible on the warehouse door across the dock. The session mic-drop: the operation belongs to Mathr, not Vane. The trail goes north.

**Use For:** Identifying the Mathr Token as the south-dock mic-drop and proof that the operation belongs to Mathr.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/mathr-token`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Aldric's Letter (the document of authority; different item; also proves Mathr's involvement), The Twelve Artefacts (the objects being moved; not the token).

**Key Relationships:**
- Aldric Vane — his housecarls wear it; he is not the authority the token indicates.
- Jarl Mathr — his sigil; the token routes the trail north to Valweg.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The mic-drop significance is player-accessible.
