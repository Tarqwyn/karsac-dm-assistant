# The Pryzi Key

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-pryzi-key`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-pryzi-key`

**Entity Type:** Item — key (Campaign 2 forward reference)

**Retrieval Summary:** The Pryzi Key opens the sealed Pryzi Mountain vault. The two Shadow-Walkers who escaped the Halvash alley fight took it from the Cobalt Soul archive. It is currently in Yantravaq possession. What is inside the vault is the opening question of Campaign 2.

**Use For:** Identifying the Pryzi Key as the item taken by Shadow-Walkers in Session 1 and the Campaign 2 forward reference.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-pryzi-key`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Scroll Case (the Campaign 1 key to the binding; distinct item and function), The Bone Disc (recovered by the players; not the key).

**Key Relationships:**
- Shadow-Walkers — took it in the Halvash alley.
- Pryzi Mountain Vault — the location it opens.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only. Players know it was taken; its destination and function are DM-layer.
