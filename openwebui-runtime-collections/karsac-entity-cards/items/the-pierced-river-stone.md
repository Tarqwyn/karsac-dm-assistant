# The Pierced River-Stone

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-pierced-river-stone`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-pierced-river-stone`

**Entity Type:** Item — artefact (seventh of twelve)

**Retrieval Summary:** A smooth stone that fits in the curve of a hand, pierced once through. Floki set it apart from the other six artefacts in the cargo cabin at dawn during the Whale-Road crossing. He did not explain why. He carries it in his coat. He has told no one. DM-only: it is the most significant of the twelve binding components, recognised by Floki from the oldest layer of Karsac oral tradition.

**Use For:** Identifying the pierced river-stone as Floki's hidden seventh artefact, distinguishing it from the bone disc and other items.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-pierced-river-stone`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Bone Disc (Vishara's symbol recovered in Halvash; not one of the twelve artefacts; carried by the party since Session 1), Brynja's Hidden Artefact (held by Brynja; distinct object), The Twelve Artefacts (the set; this is the seventh, held separately).

**Key Relationships:**
- Floki — carries it; recognises its significance from oral tradition; will not explain.
- The Twelve Artefacts — the seventh of twelve; held apart from the cargo.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only. Players do not know the seventh object exists.
