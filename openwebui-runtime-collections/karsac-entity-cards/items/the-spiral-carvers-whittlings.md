# The Spiral Carver's Whittlings

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-spiral-carvers-whittlings`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-spiral-carvers-whittlings`

**Entity Type:** Item — found object / Vishara symptom marker

**Retrieval Summary:** Scraps of wood produced each afternoon by the Carver at the Cooperage in Törweg's upper market. The spiral he carves is structurally identical to a fragment of the bone disc. He does not know why he carves. The whittlings are a Vishara symptom — the pattern propagating through him without his awareness.

**Use For:** Identifying the Carver's whittlings as the bone-disc geometry connection at the Cooperage, routing to Thread B and the geometry chain.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-spiral-carvers-whittlings`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Bone Disc (the original Vishara symbol; the whittlings match its geometry but are distinct objects), The Maw Carving (the wall pattern in the Maw; also the same geometry; a third distinct expression).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Carver — produces them compulsively.
- The Bone Disc — geometry match.
- The Maw Carving — same geometry, different location and medium.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The Vishara propagation mechanism is DM-layer.
