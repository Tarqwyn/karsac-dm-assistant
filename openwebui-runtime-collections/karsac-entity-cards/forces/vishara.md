# Vishara

**Canon File ID:** `forces/vishara`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/vishara`

**Entity Type:** Force — Instrument of Maharuq

**Retrieval Summary:** Vishara is the First Instrument of the Yantravaq — one of three intelligences shaped by proximity to Maharuq (the primordial principle of dissolution). Vishara's principle is Dissipation: the gradual removal of the edges of things without destroying them. It operates through culture (the Thinning of the Particular), political shaping (House Mathr, the Truthspeaker's thinning), and field operatives (Shadow-Walkers). It is the active threat in Campaign 1. Its fingerprints are visible in the contraction of Törweg.

**Use For:** Identifying Vishara as the Campaign 1 primary antagonist force, routing to its methodology and instruments, distinguishing it from Maharuq and the other Yantravaq.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/vishara`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Maharuq (the primordial force Vishara serves; not Vishara itself), Qathar and Sukaveth (the other two Instruments; distinct), The Yantravaq Collective (the collective of all three Instruments; Vishara is one of three), The Holding (Dhurvaq's counter-force; opposes Vishara's effects).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Thinning of the Particular — Vishara's community-scale methodology.
- House Mathr — Vishara's primary political vehicle.
- Shadow-Walkers — Vishara's field operatives.
- The Holding — the counter-force keeping Brynja and others specific.
