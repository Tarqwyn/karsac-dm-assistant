# Maharuq

**Canon File ID:** `forces/maharuq`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/maharuq`

**Entity Type:** Force — primordial

**Retrieval Summary:** Maharuq is the primordial principle of dissolution — the force that moves toward the dissolution of boundaries and the consuming of the distinction between one thing and another. Not evil, not hungry — it moves as entropy moves. The three Yantravaq Instruments (Vishara, Qathar, Sukaveth) were shaped by proximity to Maharuq into intelligences that serve its direction. The Yngondi Wastes and the Maw are locations where Maharuq's proximity has been historically highest.

**Use For:** Identifying Maharuq as the primordial dissolution force and the source of the Yantravaq, distinguishing it from Vishara and from Dhurvaq.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/maharuq`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Dhurvaq (the opposing primordial; boundary vs dissolution), Vishara (one of the Yantravaq Instruments shaped by Maharuq; not Maharuq itself), The Yantravaq Collective (the three Instruments; not Maharuq directly).

**Key Relationships:**
- Dhurvaq — the opposing primordial; boundary to Maharuq's dissolution.
- Yantravaq — the three Instruments shaped by proximity to Maharuq.
- Yngondi Wastes — a location of highest historical Maharuq proximity.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. Full primordial cosmology is DM-layer.
