# Veldrasil

**Canon File ID:** `forces/veldrasil`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/veldrasil`

**Entity Type:** Force — theological entity / cosmological structure

**Retrieval Summary:** Veldrasil is the World-Tree of Lösweg theology — simultaneously tree, spine, and axis of the universe. Not metaphor to the Löswegiann but the literal structural fact of existence. The Bay of Whales is, in the most literal theological sense, the body of one of the gods who inhabit Veldrasil's branches.

**Use For:** Identifying Veldrasil as the Lösweg cosmological World-Tree and the structural context for Lösweg theology.
