# The Dark Captains

**Canon File ID:** `forces/the-captains`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/the-captains`

**Entity Type:** Force — collective intelligence

**Retrieval Summary:** The Dark Captains are old intelligences in long relationship with Maharuq — distinct from the Yantravaq and predating the Hiding. They are currently moving artefacts north and positioning lieutenants for the Waking. Their identity is withheld in current play; players should not know what they are yet. The faction file at factions/the-captains redirects here.

**Use For:** Identifying the Dark Captains as the Maharuq-aligned force moving artefacts, distinguishing them from the Yantravaq.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/the-captains`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Yantravaq (Vishara, Qathar, Sukaveth — a different class of Maharuq-related entity), Shadow-Walkers (Vishara's field operatives; not the Captains themselves).

**Key Relationships:**
- Maharuq — the force they serve.
- The Twelve Artefacts — they are moving some of them north.
- The Waking — what they are positioning for.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only. Identity withheld in current play.
