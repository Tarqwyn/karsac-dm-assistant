# Qathar

**Canon File ID:** `forces/qathar`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/qathar`

**Entity Type:** Force — Instrument of Maharuq

**Retrieval Summary:** Qathar is the Second Instrument of the Yantravaq — Principle of Irreversibility. It works in centuries, not years. It has been preparing a threshold around King Dugweb's annual visit to Lösweg. The players are a threshold it did not set and did not anticipate. Campaign 2 primary antagonist force.

**Use For:** Identifying Qathar as the Second Instrument and the Campaign 2 force, distinguishing from Vishara (Campaign 1) and Sukaveth (Campaign 3).
