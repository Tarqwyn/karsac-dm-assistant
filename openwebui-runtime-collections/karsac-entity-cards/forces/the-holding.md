# The Holding

**Canon File ID:** `forces/the-holding`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/the-holding`

**Entity Type:** Force — active condition

**Retrieval Summary:** The Holding is Dhurvaq's principle of boundary and persistence expressed as distributed protection — small unnoticed acts that keep specific people specific in places where Vishara's smoothing should have reached them. Brynja Thorgrimsdotter is its most visible expression in Törweg. She does not know. She attributes her resistance to stubbornness.

**Use For:** Identifying the Holding as the DM-layer explanation for Brynja's resistance to Vishara, routing to Dhurvaq.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/the-holding`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Hiding (Dhurvaq's structural separation of Karsac from Exandria; distinct force), Dhurvaq (the primordial; the Holding is its expression, not Dhurvaq itself).

**Key Relationships:**
- Dhurvaq — the principle whose expression the Holding is.
- Brynja — the most visible current expression.
- Vishara — the force the Holding counteracts.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. DM-layer explanation; player-observable effect (Brynja's resistance).
