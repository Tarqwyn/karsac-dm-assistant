# Lösweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/losweg`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/losweg`

**Entity Type:** Place — region / nation

**Retrieval Summary:** Lösweg is the storm-born nation of the extreme northwest of Karsac, sealed from the south by the Stormwatch Mountains. Its people are defined by oral tradition, skaldic memory, and a vassalage of honour to Zörsdkog. The primary campaign region. Seat of government is Valweg; second city is Törweg; northernmost settlement is Kroshonok.

**Use For:** Identifying Lösweg as the campaign's primary nation, distinguishing it from individual Lösweg cities, and routing to regional detail.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/losweg`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Törweg (the specific city, hub of Chapter 2), Valweg (the seat of government within Lösweg), Bay of Whales (the great northern bay that is theologically part of Lösweg), Karsac (the wider continent).

**Key Relationships:**
- Törweg and Valweg — the two main cities; Törweg is the Chapter 2 hub, Valweg the political centre.
- Skalds — the oral-tradition keepers; structurally central to Lösweg culture.
- King Dugweb — Zörsdkog's king; visits annually; Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years.
