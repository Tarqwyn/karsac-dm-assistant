# The Divergence

**Canon File ID:** `events/the-divergence`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/events/the-divergence`

**Entity Type:** Event — historical / cosmological

**Retrieval Summary:** The Divergence refers to the Calamity and the establishment of the Divine Gate sealing the Betrayer Gods. In the noise of every boundary being redrawn, Dhurvaq added the Hiding's second layer — deepening Karsac's separation from Exandrian awareness. The Divine Gate's construction was the opportunity; Karsac's deeper sealing was its unintended consequence from the outside, and Dhurvaq's deliberate addition.

**Use For:** Identifying the Divergence as the historical event behind the Hiding's second layer and Karsac's current sealed state.
