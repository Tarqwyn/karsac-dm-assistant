# Erik Mathr

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/erik-mathr`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/erik-mathr`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major (DM-only)

**Retrieval Summary:** Erik Mathr is a former companion — a cleric of Ezrah who walked toward Vushhir five years ago and did not come back. He has been working inside Mathr's political cage from within, identifying nodes that can still be reached. He left the note that keeps Pell Duvash in Törweg and willing to testify. He is "the Cleric" in Erwing's coded message. He adopted the Mathr name as a strategic identity. Not yet revealed by name in play.

**Use For:** Identifying Erik Mathr as the resistance operative and the Cleric, distinguishing him from Jarl Mathr and House Mathr.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/erik-mathr`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Jarl Mathr (the antagonist; the jarl Erik is working against; distinct NPC), House Mathr (the constructed noble house; Erik uses the name strategically; not lineage), Pell Duvash (who received Erik's note; a distinct NPC).

**Key Relationships:**
- Pell Duvash — Erik identified him as a reachable node; the note gives Duvash permission to testify.
- The Coded Message — he is "the Cleric" the message refers to.
- Ezrah — his god; the force of liberation and naming cages.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only. Players know only of "the Cleric" from the coded message.
