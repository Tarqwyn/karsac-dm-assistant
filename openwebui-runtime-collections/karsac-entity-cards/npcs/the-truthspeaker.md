# The Truthspeaker

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/the-truthspeaker`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/the-truthspeaker`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** The Truthspeaker is Mathr's formal council voice — a real Lösweg role, a Skald speaking with their Jarl's verbal authority. This one has held it for twenty years and arrived in Törweg the day before the company. He wears a small black Ashvein pin — the same rare metal as the Shadow-Walkers' throwing spikes and Xyrrathh's phial. He arrives at the south dock to escort Aldric Vane away before Vane finishes asking questions. Ragnfrid perceives that his tradition's form is intact but its weight is gone.

**Use For:** Identifying the Truthspeaker as Mathr's council voice and the south-dock arrival NPC, routing to the Ashvein connection and the thinned-Skald detail.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/the-truthspeaker`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Aldric Vane (whom the Truthspeaker removes; a distinct NPC), Brynja Thorgrimsdotter (whom the Truthspeaker acknowledges but does not touch; distinct), Ragnfrid (the PC who perceives the thinning; not the Truthspeaker), any Mathr/Vane genealogy figure.

**Key Relationships:**
- Aldric Vane — escorted away with council authority before he finishes asking questions.
- Ashvein pin — connects him to the Shadow-Walker network.
- Ragnfrid — the PC who perceives his thinned tradition from outside.
- Brynja — recognises what the polite escort of Vane means; does not say so aloud.
