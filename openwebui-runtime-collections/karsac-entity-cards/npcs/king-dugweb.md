# King Dugweb

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/king-dugweb`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/king-dugweb`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** King Dugweb is the ancient hereditary head of Zörsdkog. He carries Kurogane. He is one of the five who stopped the Shade of Qadim al-Sharr — a credential the Löswegiann understand without translation. He visits Lösweg once a year; Jarl Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years, maintaining a constructed silence between Dugweb and the country he has come to see. Reaching Jarl Beorn before the next visit is the Chapter 2–3 political objective.

**Use For:** Identifying King Dugweb as the Zörsdkog monarch and the campaign's political hinge, routing to the dugweb-annual-visit event.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/king-dugweb`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Jarl Beorn (his ally in Lösweg; not Dugweb), Jarl Mathr (who has managed every visit; not Dugweb).

**Key Relationships:**
- Jarl Beorn — deep personal relationship; the route to getting Dugweb the truth.
- Jarl Mathr — has managed every Dugweb visit for sixty years.
- Dugweb Annual Visit — the political deadline of the Chapter 2–3 arc.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe overview. Kurogane and the Shade of Qadim al-Sharr details are not fully resolved in current Karsac canon.
