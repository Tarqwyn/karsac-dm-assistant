# Aldric Vane

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/aldric-vane`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/aldric-vane`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** Aldric Vane is thirty-three, Housecarl-Captain of Törweg for fifteen years. His father held the post for thirty years before him. He serves Jarl Mathr without question — not a villain, a loyal man who has been used. At the south dock he is supervising final cargo loading and beginning, for the first time, to ask why. The Truthspeaker arrives to remove him before he finishes asking.
