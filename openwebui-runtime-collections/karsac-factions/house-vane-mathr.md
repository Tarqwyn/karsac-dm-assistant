# House Vane-Mathr

**Canon File ID:** `factions/house-vane-mathr`
**Retrieval Summary:** Elevated to housecarl service by Sven Mathr in 1263; traceable to Havnarvik; a real Lösweg lineage five generations deep; the Vanes are not constructed — Mathr's are.

> Real Lösweg lineage; elevated to housecarl service sixty years ago; traceable to a fishing village in Havnarvik; the Vanes are not constructed — Mathr's are.

## Overview

The Vane family — latterly designated House Vane-Mathr in the Törweg council records — is a genuine Lösweg family from a fishing village three valleys south of Halvash, in the area around Havnarvik. They were elevated to housecarl service sixty years ago in a single instrument signed by the founder of House Mathr. Three generations have served since. The Vanes are real. House Mathr is not.

## Description

### The Genealogy

From the council archives: the Vane family has a proper Lösweg antecedent, traceable in oral tradition and written record through five generations. Halvor Vane (founder), Brynjar Vane, Kjald Vane, Torger Vane, Ragnar Vane — and Aldric as the current generation. Each generation served the Mathr family in housecarl capacity. The service is hereditary.

The elevation was contemporaneous with House Mathr's appearance in the records. The instrument of elevation was signed by Sven Mathr in 1263 — the same year the Mathr family appears in Lösweg records fully formed. The Vanes were already in Lösweg. They were elevated into service of a house that had no prior existence.

### The Significance

The contrast between the two genealogies — Vane's traceable, antecedented, orally-preserved; Mathr's appearing without antecedent at the same moment — is the key insight of Thread D. Serris names it plainly: *"The Vanes are real. Mathr's family are not. This is not how Lösweg families work."*

Aldric Vane is not corrupted. He is the loyal product of a loyal lineage serving what his family has always served. The tragedy of Aldric on the south dock is precisely this: he is exactly what he appears to be, and what he appears to be has been used.

### Havnarvik

The Vane ancestral home is Havnarvik — a fishing village three valleys south of Halvash, outside Törweg. This is noted in the council archive document (H_REC) and is the geographic proof of the family's legitimate Lösweg origin. See `places/havnarvik`.

## Player Knowledge

Players who investigate Thread D learn: the Vane family is real and traceable. The Mathr family is not. The elevation instrument dates both to the same year. The discrepancy is structural.

## DM-Only

Aldric does not know any of this. He has never needed to. He serves because his family has always served. When he begins to ask on the south dock — "that is forty-five years, and he does not look forty-five years older" — he is doing the arithmetic for the first time. The question is new. The Truthspeaker arrives to prevent it from becoming an answer.
