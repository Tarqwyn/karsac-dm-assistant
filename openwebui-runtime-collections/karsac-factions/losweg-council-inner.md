# Lösweg Inner Council

**Canon File ID:** `factions/losweg-council-inner`
**Retrieval Summary:** The inner council at Valweg; Mathr sits on it and has managed King Dugweb's annual visit for sixty years; Jarl Beorn leads it; deceived, not corrupted.

> The inner council at Valweg; Jarl Mathr has managed it for sixty years; Jarl Beorn leads it; the council is deceived, not corrupted.

## Overview

The Lösweg inner council sits in Valweg, the seat of government. It is the governing body of Lösweg's formal political structure — the council that manages relations with Zörsdkog, receives King Dugweb's annual visit, and coordinates the Housecarl-Captains of Törweg and other Lösweg settlements. Jarl Mathr has managed every Dugweb visit for sixty years. Jarl Beorn leads the council. Beorn is deceived by Mathr, not corrupted.

## Description

### Mathr's Role

Mathr has held a seat on the inner council for sixty years — since his family's sudden and undocumented appearance in Lösweg political life in 1263. He has served as the council's institutional memory around Dugweb's visits: managing briefings, managing the record of what Dugweb sees, and managing — carefully, for sixty years — what Dugweb does not see. He does not look forty-five years older than when Vane's father began serving him. The council has not noticed. Noticing this is Vishara's work at the political scale: the question that does not get asked.

### Jarl Beorn

Beorn leads the council. He has a deep personal relationship with King Dugweb — built across decades of annual visits, trusted by both sides. He trusts Mathr. He has trusted Mathr for sixty years and has no reason not to. He is, in Brynja's assessment: *"not corrupted. Deceived. If you can show him what you have, he will act."*

Beorn is the happy path to Dugweb. See `npcs/jarl-beorn`.

### The Dugweb Visit

Dugweb's annual visit is approaching — in seven weeks from the Chapter 2 events. Mathr has managed every visit and kept a carefully constructed silence between Dugweb and the country he has come to see. Qathar has been arranging something around this visit for sixty years. Whether the players arrive in Valweg before Dugweb determines whether they have a chance to put the truth in front of the right man. See `events/dugweb-annual-visit`.

## Player Knowledge

Players know: the inner council exists in Valweg; Mathr sits on it; Jarl Beorn leads it; Beorn is deceived not corrupted; reaching Beorn before Dugweb's visit is the route forward.

## DM-Only

The inner council is Vishara's most successfully shaped institution in Lösweg. The burned tapestries in the council chamber, the way Dugweb's visits have been managed to prevent him seeing the country clearly, the sixty-year absence of the question about Mathr's age — all of this is the Smoothing at its most complete. The council does not know what it is inside.

Qathar's arrangement around the Dugweb visit is not visible yet at the Chapter 2 stage. By the time players reach Valweg, whatever Qathar has arranged will be expressing. See `forces/qathar`.
