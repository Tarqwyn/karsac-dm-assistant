# The South Dock Confrontation

**Canon File ID:** `events/south-dock-confrontation`
**Retrieval Summary:** Scene 5; the confrontation with Aldric Vane at the south dock; the Truthspeaker's arrival; the mic-drop: the token is Mathr's, not Vane's.

> Scene 5; the confrontation with Vane; the Truthspeaker removes the consequences; the mic-drop.

## Overview

The players reach the south dock. Vane is supervising final loading. The confrontation is not with a villain — it is with a loyal man who, in this room, begins to ask why for the first time in fifteen years.

The Truthspeaker arrives shortly after the encounter resolves. Walking, not running. With council authority. To remove the consequences cleanly and reframe the session.

**The mic-drop:** not the confrontation itself. The token at the housecarls' collars — Mathr's sigil, not Vane's house mark. The operation belongs to Mathr. The trail goes north to Valweg. This is what they leave the south dock knowing.
