# The Whale-Road Crossing

**Canon File ID:** `events/whale-road-crossing`
**Retrieval Summary:** Two-day crossing from Halvash to Törweg aboard the Greyback; the cargo unveiled; the arm-ring given; the Sea Hag encounter; the Deepwhale; arrival at dawn.

> Two days aboard the Greyback; cargo unveiled; arm-ring passed; Sea Hag and Deepwhale; Törweg appears at dawn.

## Overview

The two-day crossing from Halvash to Törweg on the Greyback. The crossing is strange in different registers on each day. The Halvashi pilot navigates by things the players cannot perceive.

Day one: cargo unveiled in the cabin (six of seven objects; the seventh set apart by Floki without explanation); Floki's map with the marked square — the Maw; the Sea Hag encounter mid-afternoon, drawn by the cargo; the Deepwhale's intervention; Floki afterward at the rail looking at the water where the body went down. The morning before departure: the arm-ring passed from Floki to Ragnfrid at the prow as Törweg first appears in the mist.

Day two: quieter; the water its ordinary colour; the pilot at the tiller with careful gestures; Floki on the foredeck looking east. By dusk, the Stormwatch Mountains on the horizon. They arrive at dawn of day three.

## Significance

The crossing is where the cargo becomes real to the players. The Deepwhale acknowledged what was aboard — something old recognising the binding components. Floki goes still at the foredeck. Nobody has slept well since the cargo came aboard.
