# The Duvash Assassination Attempt

**Canon File ID:** `events/duvash-attack`
**Retrieval Summary:** Thread C; ten to fifteen minutes into Duvash's testimony, two assassins appear at the back of the building; Duvash goes very still — he is not surprised.

> Thread C; assassins arrive during Duvash's testimony; he is not surprised; confirms someone wants the conversation stopped.

## Overview

Thread C in Scene 4. Midway through Duvash's testimony, two figures appear at the back of the building — they did not come through the front. Duvash goes very still. He is not surprised. He has been expecting something like this.

The assassins confirm what Duvash already knew: he has been kept in place by a note from someone watching out for him, and someone else has noticed he hasn't fled.

Initiative is called. Duvash is between the players and the assassins.

On the happy path, after the fight: *"Get me out of Törweg. I will go wherever you go next. I will not stay here another night."*
