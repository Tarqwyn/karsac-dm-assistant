# The Divergence

**Canon File ID:** `events/the-divergence`
**Retrieval Summary:** The Calamity and its aftermath; the establishment of the Divine Gate; the moment Dhurvaq used to extend the Hiding's second layer; sealed the Betrayer Gods and, unintentionally, deepened Karsac's separation.

> The Calamity's aftermath; the Divine Gate established; Dhurvaq added the Hiding's second layer in the noise of every boundary being redrawn.

## Overview

The Divergence is the historical event following the Calamity in which the prime deities established the Divine Gate to seal the Betrayer Gods from direct action in the mortal world, and simultaneously withdrew behind it themselves. It is foundational theological history across Exandria.

Its second, unintended function: at the moment of the Gate's establishment, Dhurvaq used the noise of every boundary being redrawn to extend the Hiding's separation of Karsac from Exandrian awareness — more thoroughly than it had previously been able to achieve. The gods did not notice. They were not attending to Karsac during the Divergence.
