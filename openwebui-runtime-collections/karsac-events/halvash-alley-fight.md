# The Halvash Alley Fight

**Canon File ID:** `events/halvash-alley-fight`
**Retrieval Summary:** Session 1 ending; three Shadow-Walkers at the Cobalt Soul archive; one killed, two escaped with the Pryzi key; the bone disc recovered; the boy died.

> Three Shadow-Walkers; one dead; two escaped with the Pryzi key; the bone disc recovered; the boy died.

## Overview

The fight that ended Session 1 and began Session 2. Three Shadow-Walkers came to the Halvash Cobalt Soul archive. One died in the alley. Two escaped — by launch, to a Lösweg fishing trawler sitting three miles off the eastern coast. The trawler went dark as soon as they reached it.

The boy was removed by Edvar's people, who worked without speaking.

The bone disc is in someone's pocket. The sealed document — the Pryzi manifest — is folded inside Rowan's coat, written in Veyr, partially legible to Ragnfrid. Edvar Solm has not slept.

## What Was Won, What Was Lost

**Won:** the bone disc (recovered from the dead Shadow-Walker); the company's decision to board the Greyback rather than take the Windmere west; Ragnfrid.

**Lost:** the Pryzi key (the Shadow-Walkers took it); the Cobalt Soul archive's seal (broken); the boy.

The throwing spikes were Ashvein — the same material as the Truthspeaker's pin. The players will not know the significance of that material until the south dock.
