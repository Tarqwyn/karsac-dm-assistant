# Stormwatch Foothill Path, Törweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/torweg/stormwatch-foothill-path`
**Retrieval Summary:** The eastern exit from Törweg, leading up the Stormwatch foothills to the Maw; two hours on foot.

> The eastern exit from Törweg; the path up the Stormwatch foothills to the Maw; two hours on foot.

## Overview

The foothill path leads east from Törweg into the Stormwatch Mountains, connecting the city to the Maw — the fracture site that opened itself three months ago. It is approximately two hours on foot. The Stormwatch Mountains are visible from the city as a dark line rising immediately east.

## Description

The path is the route Thread B takes. Players who want to investigate the Maw follow it east from the city and climb the foothills until they reach the fracture entrance. The path is passable in daylight and is how Vane's housecarls moved artefacts down from the Maw when the vault opened.

There is nothing remarkable about the path itself beyond what it connects: Törweg below and the Maw above. The transition from town to mountain happens quickly. By the time the city is below them and the silence of the foothills is around them, players have already left the warm-town register and entered something older.
