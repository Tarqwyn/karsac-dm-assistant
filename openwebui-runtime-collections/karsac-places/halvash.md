# Halvash

**Canon File ID:** `places/halvash`
**Retrieval Summary:** The anomalous port on Karsac's coast; controlled point of contact with Exandria; Cobalt Soul archive; the company's last ground before the Whale-Road.

> The anomalous port on Karsac's coast; the unique known point of regular contact between Karsac and Exandria; home of a Cobalt Soul archive.

## Overview

Halvash is the inconvenient case. By every standard used to describe other ports in the Sourcebook, it should not be as widely known as it is. Karsac is famously difficult of approach; its sea-roads vary year by year; the better part of the continent has gone unmapped within Exandrian memory. Yet Halvash is known. Ships put in. Records arrive. Scholars meet. It is the working anomaly — the controlled point of contact.

## Description

### The Anomaly

The Sourcebook's Chapter 7 (forthcoming) explicitly frames Halvash as a question to be examined with attention to the Cobalt Soul presence, the Löswegiann use of its harbour, and the particular silences that attend the question of how Halvash became the controlled point of contact it has. Whether Halvash represents an intentional exception in the Hiding's structure or a gap Dhurvaq cannot close is not resolved in available sources.

### In Play — Session 1 and the Session 2 Opener

The company spent time in Halvash before the Whale-Road crossing. The alley fight — three Shadow-Walkers, the boy's death, the bone disc recovered, the Pryzi key taken — occurred here. See `events/halvash-alley-fight`.

The night before sailing, the company gathered at the Meridian. Edvar Solm came to the door. He said: *"I have been the harbourmaster of Halvash for nineteen years. I have never had to think about the Yngondi Wastes. I am asking you to find out what is wrong in Lösweg. I am asking because I do not know who else to ask."* Then he went back to his post.

At dawn, the Windmere sailed west. The Greyback sailed east. The company was aboard the Greyback.

### The Cobalt Soul Archive

Serris operates from the Halvash archive. The Cobalt Soul's presence here is the closest any organisation has come to maintaining a deliberate record of the Hiding's fragments — knowledge of Karsac, documents that do not connect, a pattern that someone like Serris has been approaching for three years without quite reaching it.

The archive was broken into during the alley fight. The Pryzi key was inside it.

## Atmosphere & Themes

Halvash is the door between worlds — the one place the Hiding permits contact. Everything the company brought through that door is now in Karsac: the bone disc, the Pryzi manifest, the scroll case, themselves. The Windmere sailed west. The west closed. The only road that remains is east, into the silence in Törweg.
