# South Dock Warehouse, Törweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/torweg/south-dock-warehouse`
**Retrieval Summary:** Warehouse where the Truthspeaker holds court at session-end; where Vane is taken after the escort.

> Where the Truthspeaker holds court at session-end; not a player-accessible location.

## Overview

The south dock warehouse is the operational space the Truthspeaker uses at session-end. It is not a location the players access. It is where Aldric Vane is taken after the polite escort.

## Description

What happens in the warehouse is not depicted in available sources — only its function. Vane is going to be silenced. He has begun to ask questions and Mathr cannot have a Housecarl-Captain who asks questions. The polite escort is the thing Brynja flinches at when she hears about it. She knows.

The warehouse carries Vane's house mark. The token the housecarls wear does not match it. This discrepancy is one of the tells available at the south dock.

## DM-Only

This location exists primarily to give the Truthspeaker a functional base of operations in Törweg. The specific nature of Vane's "retirement" — whether killed, memory-altered, or genuinely retired to an estate in the north — is DM discretion. Brynja's assessment: *"He will not be questioned at council. He will be thanked, honoured, and quietly retired to an estate in the north and not seen again. That is how it is done."* She says this without believing the estate version.
