# Pryzi Mountain Vault

**Canon File ID:** `places/pryzi-mountain-vault`
**Retrieval Summary:** Sealed vault that the Shadow-Walkers' key opens; what is inside is the opening question of Campaign 2; currently the key is in Yantravaq possession.

> Sealed vault that the Shadow-Walkers' key opens; what is inside is the opening question of Campaign 2.

## Overview

The Pryzi Mountain Vault is a sealed location — its contents unknown, its significance Campaign-2 scope. The Shadow-Walkers came to Halvash in Session 1 to recover the key from the Cobalt Soul archive. They succeeded. Two escaped with the key; one died in the alley. The key is currently in Yantravaq possession.

This entity is not yet known to the players.

## Description

Location, physical description, and contents are not specified in available Campaign 1 sources. What is known:

- The key the Shadow-Walkers extracted from Halvash opens this vault.
- The Yantravaq wanted it badly enough to mount an operation across two continents to retrieve it.
- Whatever is inside is the opening question of Campaign 2.
- Whether the players recover the key during Campaign 1 determines how much leverage they carry into Campaign 2.

### Two Paths Forward

If the players recover the key: they have access to the vault and whatever is inside becomes the opening question of Campaign 2.

If they do not: the Yantravaq have it, and the opening question of Campaign 2 is what was taken and what it means that the wrong hands have it.

Either way the key is the thread forward. Something is always still in play.

## DM-Only

The vault's contents are not specified in Campaign 1 sources. The storyarch author withholds them as Campaign 2 material. The campaign carries at least two threads into Campaign 2 that may connect to the vault: the Pryzi key itself, and the Air Ashari connection (what Korvann's settlement was protecting; what all three Instruments want to contain or reach). Whether these connect to the vault's contents is not confirmed in current sources.

## Ambiguities

- Location within Karsac is not specified (only "Pryzi Mountain" as a general reference).
- Contents are Campaign 2 scope — not specified in current corpus.
