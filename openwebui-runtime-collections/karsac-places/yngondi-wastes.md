# The Yngondi Wastes

**Canon File ID:** `places/yngondi-wastes`
**Retrieval Summary:** Eastern unmapped territory; Vushhir maintains records, warnings, silences, and several sealed files; the oldest stratum of Karsac oral tradition; where the Captains are moving artefacts.

> Eastern unmapped territory; Vushhir maintains records, warnings, silences, and several sealed files.

## Overview

The Yngondi Wastes lie east and north of the Eastern Reaches, past the limits of any chart Vushhir has been able to verify with confidence. Vushhir's relationship to this territory is characterised by reluctance: *"Here the scholar must proceed carefully."* There are records. There are testimonies. There are battlefield accounts, refugee songs, military reports, Zörsdkogi claims, Barbyturan warnings, Vushhiri field observations, and several sealed files whose seals are not lightly disturbed.

## Description

### The Sourcebook's Caution

The Preface addresses Yngondi directly — the only territory given a section of its own there — because to say what happened in Yngondi is difficult. Too many powers acted there. Too much was destroyed. Too much survived in altered form. The land itself appears to have become an unreliable witness. Distances behave strangely in certain accounts. Bodies were not always found where they fell. Several reports from disciplined military observers contain descriptions that later copyists attempted to remove as madness.

The proto-Yngondi cultures — the peoples whose oral tradition would eventually become the oldest stratum of the continental memory — are also the cultures from whom the Shadow-Walkers trace their deepest roots. From those oldest cultures, Vishara's oldest symbol emerged.

### Current Relevance

The Captains are currently moving artefacts from the Stormwatch Mountains **north toward the Yngondi**. This is the direction of their current preparation. What the Yngondi Wastes represent as a destination — what is there, what the Captains are moving toward — is not specified in available sources. See `forces/the-captains`.

Vushhir maintains records, warnings, silences, and several sealed files about Yngondi. The sealed files are the most informative in the inventory — the Sourcebook says so directly without opening them.

## Player Knowledge

Players may know:

- The Yngondi Wastes are east of the Eastern Reaches, past reliable charting.
- Vushhir is cautious about them. Deliberately cautious.
- The oldest Karsac oral traditions — including those that carry Vishara's oldest symbol — trace to cultures that once inhabited this region.

- The specific nature of what the Captains are moving *toward* in the Yngondi is not stated.
