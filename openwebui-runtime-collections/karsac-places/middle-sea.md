# The Middle Sea

**Canon File ID:** `places/middle-sea`
**Retrieval Summary:** The body of water between Vushhir and the wider Exandrian world; the primary trade and diplomatic sea-road connecting Karsac to the south.

> The body of water between Vushhir and the wider world; the primary sea-road connecting Karsac to Exandria.

## Overview

The Middle Sea lies between Vushhir and the wider world, serving as the primary trade and diplomatic route connecting Karsac to Exandrian civilisations. Caravans and ships from across Karsac converge on Vugasir via the Middle Sea; roads of pale stone stretch from Vugasir toward its shores.

## Description

The Middle Sea is characterised in the Sourcebook primarily through Vushhir's geographic positioning: roads stretch from Vugasir outward toward it, carrying copper, spices, timber, glass, ink, vellum, surgical instruments, star charts, and more rumours than can be properly catalogued in either direction.

Beyond its role as Vushhir's sea-facing trade route, the Middle Sea does not receive extended treatment in the current sourcebook. It is a geographic constant rather than a thematic entity.
