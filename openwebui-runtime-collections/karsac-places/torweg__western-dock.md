# Western Dock, Törweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/torweg/western-dock`
**Retrieval Summary:** The Greyback's berth; site of the company's arrival and Brynja's first words; the man with the black pin is at the far end.

> The Greyback's berth; site of the company's arrival and Brynja's first words.

## Overview

The western dock is where the Greyback moors on arrival in Törweg. It is the point of first contact with the city — where the flag at the harbourmaster's tower is visible, where the wrong number of people on the wharf is first apparent, where Brynja is waiting, and where the man with the black pin stands at the far end watching without being seen to watch.

## Description

Dawn of day three. The Greyback comes in along the western dock. Three figures on the wharf where there should be twenty. They are not unloading anything. They are watching.

Brynja Thorgrimsdotter: older, broad-shouldered, in seal-hide. She meets Floki's eyes and the smallest thing happens in her face. She steps forward as the gangplank goes down. She does not embrace him. They have known each other twenty years.

*"Floki. You came back with strangers. Why."*

At the far end of the wharf: the man in the long dark coat with the small black pin at his throat. He has been there as long as Brynja has. He is not unloading anything. He is not watching anyone in particular. He is, somehow, watching everything.

## Player Knowledge

Players arrive here at the start of Scene 3. They see the flag at half-mast, the wrong number of people, Brynja waiting, and the man with the black pin. Brynja's first words are delivered here. The invitation to come somewhere with walls follows.

## DM-Only

The man with the black pin is the Truthspeaker. He arrived from Valweg the day before. He knows the company is coming — someone in Mathr's network warned him the Greyback was incoming with strangers. He does not approach yet. He is assessing. Brynja knows he is there and has decided not to acknowledge him publicly.
