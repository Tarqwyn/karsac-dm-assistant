# Isle of Mist

**Canon File ID:** `places/isle-of-mist`
**Retrieval Summary:** Inconvenient island off Lösweg; partially mapped; fogs that defy charting; legend holds the Old People still live there.

> Inconvenient island off Lösweg; fogs that defy charting; the last refuge of the Old People.

## Overview

Visible from the northern headlands on clear days — and clear days are rare — the Isle of Mist occupies a peculiar position in both the geography and imagination of Lösweg. It is known. It has been sailed to, settled fitfully, and partially mapped. And yet it remains, in every meaningful sense, a mystery.

## Description

The most seasoned Löswegiann sailors speak of the Isle with a particular quality of voice — not fear exactly, but something adjacent to it. A respect that borders on dread. The crossing from the mainland is treacherous in ways that defy consistent charting; the fogs appear and vanish without reference to wind or season, and more than one expedition has reported arriving at a coastline that did not match their previous knowledge of the place.

The permanent settlements on the Isle are few and self-reliant to a degree remarkable even by Lösweg standards. Those who choose to winter there speak of an isolation so complete it becomes, eventually, something like clarity. Several of Lösweg's most celebrated skalds are said to have made the crossing alone and returned months later fundamentally changed — their gift sharpened to something that unsettled even those who knew them.

### The Old People

Legend holds — and this is a legend that even senior Löswegiann do not dismiss — that the Isle is the last refuge of the Old People: a race or lineage that predates the current inhabitants of Lösweg, perhaps predates Karsac itself. The Vushhiri faculty has debated vigorously whether the Old People are mythic, extinct, or genuinely present in some form on the Isle. They record the legend faithfully and make no determination.

Three Vushhiri scholars who pressed this question directly with Löswegiann hosts report the same experience: their hosts became suddenly and completely unwilling to discuss the matter further. One was politely but unmistakably escorted to his lodgings. The faculty considers this, in its own way, informative.

## Player Knowledge

Players may know: the Isle of Mist exists, is visible from the northern headlands on clear days, is partially mapped, and is associated with the Old People in Lösweg tradition. Senior Löswegiann treat the question of whether the Old People are still there as one not to press.
