## Overview

Urdid is a dwarf who travels with the company. The BETA notes his inclusion as conditional on the player's attendance. If absent, a protocol in the BETA appendix places him in-world without the ill-dwarf trope.

## Description

The BETA gives Urdid one specific character beat: in the cargo cabin at dawn, after the seven objects are unwrapped, he lingers. He is a dwarf. The cargo has weight. He notices weight. He does not say why he stays.

Brynja's hook for him at the hall is specific: *"The Greyback is moored. The cargo is aboard. Floki trusts no one with it. If you have a mind to sit with it while we walk the city, he will not refuse. There is weight to those things. You will feel what the others walk past."*

This dovetails with his sensitivity to the cargo's physical reality. He can guard what others walk past. He can also walk Törweg with the company — both are valid.
