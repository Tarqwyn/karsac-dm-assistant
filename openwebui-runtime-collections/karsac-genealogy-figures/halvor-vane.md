> Founder of the Vane housecarl line; elevated to housecarl service by Sven Mathr in 1263; traceable to Havnarvik.

Halvor Vane is the founding figure of the Vane housecarl lineage. His elevation to housecarl service — in the instrument signed by Sven Mathr in 1263 — is the founding document that established three generations of Vane service to the Mathr family. The archivist's note traces his lineage to Havnarvik. He is the proof that the Vane family is real: antecedented, oral-historically present, a genuine Lösweg fishing family elevated into service.
