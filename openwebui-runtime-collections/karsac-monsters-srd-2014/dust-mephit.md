# Dust Mephit

**Rule ID:** `monsters/srd-2014/dust-mephit`

## Mechanical Summary

Dust Mephit is a Small elemental (neutral evil) at CR 1/2 worth 100 XP. It has AC 12, 17 (5d6) hit points, and speed 30 ft., fly 30 ft.. In play it fits controller, hazard, shock-assault use, with key traits such as Death Burst, Innate Spellcasting (1/Day) and actions such as Claws, Blinding Breath (Recharge 6).

## Stat Block

- **Size/Type/Alignment:** Small elemental, neutral evil
- **Armor Class:** 12
- **Hit Points:** 17 (5d6)
- **Speed:** 30 ft., fly 30 ft.
- **Ability Scores:** STR 5 (-3), DEX 14 (+2), CON 10 (+0), INT 9 (-1), WIS 11 (+0), CHA 10 (+0)
- **Skills:** Perception +2, Stealth +4
- **Damage Vulnerabilities:** fire
- **Damage Immunities:** poison
- **Condition Immunities:** poisoned
- **Senses:** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
- **Languages:** Auran, Terran
- **Challenge:** 1/2 (100 XP)

### Traits

- **Death Burst.** When the mephit dies, it explodes in a burst of dust. Each creature within 5 feet of it must then succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or be blinded for 1 minute. A blinded creature can repeat the saving throw on each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
- **Innate Spellcasting (1/Day).** The mephit can innately cast sleep, requiring no material components. Its innate spellcasting ability is Charisma.

### Actions

- **Claws.** Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.
- **Blinding Breath (Recharge 6).** The mephit exhales a 15-- foot cone of blinding dust. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 10 Dexterity saving throw or be blinded for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
