## Mechanical Summary

In play it fits shock-assault, controller, corruptor use, with key traits such as Keen Hearing and Smell, Pack Tactics and actions such as Bite, Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6).

## Stat Block

- **Size/Type/Alignment:** Medium fiend, lawful evil
- **Armor Class:** 15 (natural armor)
- **Hit Points:** 45 (7d8 + 14)
- **Speed:** 50 ft.
- **Ability Scores:** STR 17 (+3), DEX 12 (+1), CON 14 (+2), INT 6 (-2), WIS 13 (+1), CHA 6 (-2)
- **Skills:** Perception +5
- **Damage Immunities:** fire
- **Senses:** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 15
- **Languages:** understands Infernal but can't speak it
- **Challenge:** 3 (700 XP)

### Traits

- **Keen Hearing and Smell.** The hound has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.
- **Pack Tactics.** The hound has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the hound's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

### Actions

- **Bite.** Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d8 + 3) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage.
- **Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6).** The hound exhales fire in a 15--foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
