# Bonus Actions

**Rule ID:** `rules/core/bonus-actions`

**Retrieval Summary:** A bonus action exists only when a spell, class feature, or other rule specifically gives you one.

## Rule Summary

You can take at most one bonus action on your turn, and only if a rule says you can.

## Procedure

1. Check whether a feature, spell, or other rule grants a bonus action.
2. If it does, choose whether to use it during your turn unless the rule fixes the timing.
3. Resolve the bonus action.
4. Do not take another bonus action that turn.

## At the Table

Use this when players try to fit extra actions into a turn or combine several bonus-action features.

## Common Questions

### Do I always get a bonus action?

No. If no rule grants one, there is no bonus action to spend.

### Can I take a bonus action if I cannot take actions?

No. Anything that removes your ability to take actions also stops you from taking bonus actions.
