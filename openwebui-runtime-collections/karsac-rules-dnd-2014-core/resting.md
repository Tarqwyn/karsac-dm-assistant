# Resting

**Rule ID:** `rules/core/resting`

**Retrieval Summary:** A short rest is at least 1 hour and lets characters spend Hit Dice, while a long rest is at least 8 hours and restores hit points and some spent Hit Dice.

## Rule Summary

Rest resets resources, heals damage, and refreshes spellcasting and endurance features that say they return on a rest.

## Procedure

1. For a short rest, spend at least 1 hour doing only light activity.
2. At the end of a short rest, spend Hit Dice one at a time and regain rolled hit points plus Constitution each time.
3. For a long rest, spend at least 8 hours resting, with no more than 2 hours of light activity and no interruption by at least 1 hour of strenuous activity.
4. At the end of a long rest, regain all lost hit points.
5. Also regain spent Hit Dice equal to up to half your total Hit Dice, minimum one.

## At the Table

Use this when the party pauses to heal, recover class resources, or asks whether an interrupted rest still counts.

## Common Questions

### How often can a creature benefit from a long rest?

No more than once in a 24-hour period.

### Can a creature at 0 hit points benefit from a long rest?

No. A creature must have at least 1 hit point at the start of the rest to gain its benefits.
