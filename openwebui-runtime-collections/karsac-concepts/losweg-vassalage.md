# Lösweg Vassalage to Zörsdkog

**Canon File ID:** `concepts/losweg-vassalage`
**Retrieval Summary:** Lösweg is formally vassal to Zörsdkog; the vassalage is of honour rather than submission; Lösweg chose it because Dugweb is the kind of leader its theology honours.

> A vassalage of honour, not submission; Lösweg chose it because Dugweb is the kind of leader its theology can admire without translation.

## Overview

Lösweg is formally a vassal state of Zörsdkog. This is a relationship of admiration rather than conquest. The Löswegiann honour gods who know they will die and face it anyway — they honour a king by the same logic. Dugweb's personal authority and legendary history made the vassalage legible to the Löswegiann in a way that most political relationships are not.

In practice: ships, sailors, and fighting capacity flow to Zörsdkog; trade access and political stability flow back. Dugweb visits once a year. The relationship has held for generations.

## Mathr's Management

Jarl Mathr has managed every annual visit for sixty years, keeping a careful silence between Dugweb and the country he has come to see. Brynja's read: if Dugweb saw clearly what Mathr has managed him not to see, Dugweb would act. He is not complicit. He is managed.
