## Overview

The Old People are a legendary lineage said to predate the current inhabitants of Lösweg — perhaps Karsac itself. The Isle of Mist is their last refuge, according to Lösweg tradition.

Whether the Old People are mythic, extinct, or genuinely present on the Isle is explicitly left undetermined in the Sourcebook. Three Vushhiri scholars who pressed this question directly with Löswegiann hosts reported the same experience: their hosts became suddenly and completely unwilling to discuss the matter further. One was politely but unmistakably escorted to his lodgings.

The Vushhiri faculty considers this, in its own way, informative.
