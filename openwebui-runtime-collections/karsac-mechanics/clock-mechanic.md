# The Clock Mechanic

**Canon File ID:** `mechanics/clock-mechanic`
**Retrieval Summary:** The silent pressure clock for Chapter 2 Törweg investigation; tracks Vane's awareness that strangers are asking; never announced to players; increments and tiers.

> Chapter 2 Törweg; a silent clock tracking Vane's awareness; never announced; communicated through the city's behaviour.

## Overview

The clock runs silently through Scenes 4 and 5. Never announced. Communicated through Törweg's behaviour: doors closing a beat too quickly, conversations ending when players approach, a man on a corner who was there ten minutes ago who is gone.

The clock has no numbers. The DM tracks it privately.

## Increments

Clock advances by **one** for: public questions, moving in obvious groups, drawing attention in the mead hall, lingering at the harbourmaster's office.

Clock advances by **two** for: failed subterfuge checks, being seen near the south dock before Scene 5, drawing a weapon in the open, starting combat in the streets.

## Tiers

| Tier | State | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| Low | Vane's people watching but uncertain | Players move freely |
| Medium | Vane has been told strangers are asking; Sygna's description is in the air | Increased attention |
| High | Housecarls positioned at south dock approaches; streets have eyes on the company | Approach is difficult |
| Critical | Vane has decided the operation must complete before he speaks to anyone; accelerating loading | Two clock per round of street combat; each increment = another crate aboard |
