# Pell Duvash

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/pell-duvash`
**Retrieval Summary:** Trading-house merchant; intermediary for three transactions of very old objects moving south; kept in place by a note from Erik; past softening.

> Trading-house merchant; intermediary for three artefact transactions; kept in place by a note from Erik Mathr; past the point of softening.

## Overview

Pell Duvash is the Thread C NPC — the merchant in Törweg's upper market who has been the intermediary for three consignments of artefacts moving south to Bruithwr. He did not know what he was handling at first. He does now. A note in a place only he would look told him: *"Stay, and tell the truth to whoever comes."* He has been waiting.

## Description

Mid-fifties. Soft hands. Quiet eyes. He has the specific quality of a man who has been awake for too long and has made his decisions. He is past softening.

When the players sit down with him: *"Sit. Please. I have been waiting for someone who would actually listen."*

Three transactions. The first two letters from Aldric's hand. The third: seal correct, signature correct, instruction too direct. Vane has been careful for fifteen years. The last instruction was not careful. Someone else wrote it. The captain collecting was a Bruithwr man named Kohlmar.

He keeps the note in a drawer. He has not destroyed it because the note told him to tell the truth to whoever comes, and destroying the note would be the opposite of that.

## Voice & Bearing

Past softening. Sounds like a man who has made his decision and is waiting for the moment to act on it. Not dramatic — resigned is not quite right either. He is someone who has been carrying a thing he should not be carrying, who received permission to put it down, and who is now doing so.

## Lines to Inhabit

- *"Three transactions. The first two letters were Aldric's writing. The third—"* (looks up) *"—the third was not. The seal was right. The signature was right. The instruction was too direct."*
- *"Vane has been careful for fifteen years. The last instruction was not careful. Someone else wrote it."*
- *"The captain collecting was a Bruithwr man called Kohlmar."*
- *(After the assassins, if alive:)* *"Get me out of Törweg. I have given you what I have. I will go wherever you go next. I will not stay here another night."*

## Motivations

**What he wants:** to tell someone what he has been carrying. To survive. To no longer be in Törweg.

**What he hides:** nothing, by the time the players reach him. The note gave him permission to disclose. He is disclosing.

## DM-Only

The note was left by Erik Mathr — the cleric of Ezrah working inside Mathr's network. Erik identified Duvash as a node who could still be reached, who had not been fully smoothed, and who would be useful when someone arrived asking the right questions. The assassins who arrive ten to fifteen minutes into the conversation were sent by whoever noticed that Duvash had not fled Törweg as he should have by now.
