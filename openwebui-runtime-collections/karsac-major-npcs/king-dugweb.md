> Hereditary head of Zörsdkog; ancient; of legendary personal authority; visits Lösweg once a year; Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years.

## Overview

King Dugweb is the hereditary head of Zörsdkog. He is ancient. He carries Kurogane. He is one of the five people who stopped the Shade of Qadim al-Sharr — a credential that the Löswegiann understand without translation. He visits Lösweg once a year, and Jarl Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years, keeping a carefully constructed silence between Dugweb and the country he has come to see.

## Description

The Löswegiann relationship to Dugweb is defined precisely by the sourcebook's Chapter 2: a people who honour their gods precisely because those gods chose to face an unwinnable war with dignity find in Dugweb something they understand at a level that requires no translation. This is not submission. It is the respect of the capable for the extraordinary.

Dugweb's annual visit is the political hinge of the campaign's Chapter 2–3 arc. He will visit in seven weeks. Mathr has arranged every previous visit. If the players reach Jarl Beorn before the visit, there is a chance to put the truth in front of Dugweb before Mathr can shape what he sees.
