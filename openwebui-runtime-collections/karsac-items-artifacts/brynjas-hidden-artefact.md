# Brynja's Hidden Artefact

**Canon File ID:** `items/brynjas-hidden-artefact`
**Retrieval Summary:** One of the twelve artefacts; hidden by Brynja behind a stone in her hearth; she does not know what it is, only that it should not leave.

> One of the twelve artefacts; behind a stone in Brynja's hearth; she does not know what it is.

## Overview

One of the original twelve artefacts from the Stormwatch vault, held by Brynja Thorgrimsdotter. On the day the objects came down from the Maw, she kept this one. She does not know what it is. She knows it should not leave. She cannot explain this knowing.

It reacted at the session's close — a pulse of warmth felt at her hearth when the recovered artefacts were near the south dock — before she knew the south dock confrontation had even happened.

## Description

Physical description is not given in available sources — it is one of the twelve objects, therefore consistent with the set (plain, heavy, unremarkable to look at, warm to the touch). Its specific form is DM discretion.

On the happy path, at the session close, Brynja removes the hearthstone herself and places the artefact on the table. She does not explain. She does not need to.
