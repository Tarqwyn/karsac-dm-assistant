# Mathr's Token

**Canon File ID:** `items/mathr-token`
**Retrieval Summary:** Bronze token worn at the collar of Vane's housecarls; the sigil is Mathr's, not Vane's house mark — the operation belongs to Mathr, not Vane. The session mic-drop.

> Flat bronze token, palm-sized, pierced once; worn by Vane's housecarls; sigil is Mathr's, not Vane's — the trail goes north.

## Overview

Each of Vane's housecarls at the south dock wears a small bronze token at their collar — flat, palm-sized, pierced once for a leather thong. The sigil on it does not match the Vane house mark visible on the warehouse door across the dock.

The sigil is Mathr's.

The housecarls on the south dock are wearing the Jarl's mark because they are operating under the Jarl's direct order. Vane is not the authority. He never was. He was the face. The operation belonged to Mathr.

## The Mic-Drop

This is the session's central revelation. Not the confrontation with Vane — Vane is exactly what he appears to be. The token is what reveals that the operation belongs to Mathr and the trail goes north to Valweg.

Serris, on a passive Investigation, can name it: *"That mark is on three documents in the council archive. All under Jarl Mathr's seal. This is Mathr's token. Why are Vane's housecarls carrying Mathr's token."*

Hold the question. Let the table answer it themselves.
