# Floki's Coastal Chart

**Canon File ID:** `items/floki-coastal-chart`

> Lösweg work from memory; marks the Whale-Road and a small square in the foothills: "the place that opened." Handout H1.

## Overview

A folded chart of Lösweg work, the western coastline of Karsac drawn from a sailor's memory rather than a surveyor's instrument. Floki spreads it on the cabin table, points to Törweg, the line of the Whale-Road, and then to a place inland where someone has marked a single small square.

He taps the square twice. Looks at Sygna.

*"The place that opened. The Maw."*

He does not say more. He says some things are best understood by walking up to them in daylight rather than discussing them on a ship. He folds it and puts it inside his coat. Time to sail.
