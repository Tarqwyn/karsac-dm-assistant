# Aldric's Letter of Authority

**Canon File ID:** `items/aldric-letter`
**Retrieval Summary:** The most recent letter of authority given to Aldric Vane; seal is Mathr's, body text is in a different hand than Mathr's signature; names Captain Kohlmar in Bruithwr. Handout H16.

> The most recent letter from Mathr to Vane; the seal is right, the signature is right, the handwriting in the body is not Mathr's. Handout H16.

## Overview

The most recent letter of authority given to Aldric Vane — found on his body if killed, or recoverable from the south dock warehouse. The first two letters in the Duvash transaction were in Aldric's description written by Mathr himself. The third was not. The seal is correct. The signature is correct. The body text is in a different hand.

This is the tell that Duvash noticed: *"Vane has been careful for fifteen years. The last instruction was not careful. Someone else wrote it."* Vane has not noticed the discrepancy. He reads orders, not calligraphy.

The letter names Captain Kohlmar in Bruithwr as the recipient.

## Player Knowledge

If recovered: the body text handwriting discrepancy; Kohlmar named; the seal is Mathr's. This extends the trail from Vane to Mathr and from Mathr south to Bruithwr.
