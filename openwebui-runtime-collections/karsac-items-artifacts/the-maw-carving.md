# The Maw Carving (Extended Bone Disc Pattern)

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-maw-carving`
**Retrieval Summary:** The extended bone disc pattern scored on the Maw's walls; Serris's recognition handout (H13 / extended bone disc pattern); the pattern continues past where the disc ends.

> The pattern on the Maw walls continues the bone disc geometry; Serris recognises it. Handout H13.

## Overview

The Maw's shaft walls are scored with the same pattern as the bone disc the company has been carrying since Session 1 — continued past the point where the disc's carving ends. This is the Maw's central discovery: the pattern is not unique to the disc. The disc is one face. The Maw walls show more.

Serris, seeing it: *"The Survey notes that Halvash exists and that no one has satisfactorily explained why Halvash is known when Karsac is not..."* — then, specifically to the wall carving: she goes very quiet.

Post handout H13 (Maw carving image) when a PC picks up the Aeorian note inside the Maw, or when Serris recognises the wall carving — the BETA specifies the sequencing.
