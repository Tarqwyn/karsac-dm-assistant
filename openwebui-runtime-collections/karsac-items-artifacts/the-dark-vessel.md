# The Dark Vessel

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-dark-vessel`
**Retrieval Summary:** Xyrrathh's dark vessel; Ashvein, neither glass nor metal; contains acid suspending a black shifting substance linked to Maharuq; two interpretations, both open.

> Xyrrathh's phial; Ashvein; acid suspending something that does not dissolve and sometimes becomes still near old marks.

## Overview

A small vessel, dark and dense, made of Ashvein — the same material as the Truthspeaker's pin and the Shadow-Walkers' throwing spikes. Volcanic in colour, cold to the touch. Within it: acid suspending a black, shifting substance that does not dissolve, does not settle, and sometimes becomes still near certain old marks.

Xyrrathh does not know whether the acid is a weapon, a prison, or preservation. He knows the vessel is evidence.

## DM-Only

The substance is linked to Maharuq. Two open interpretations — see `pcs/xyrrathh` DM-Only section for full detail. The substance responds to things it should not. Xyrrathh does not fully understand why. Leave the question open until the campaign is ready.
