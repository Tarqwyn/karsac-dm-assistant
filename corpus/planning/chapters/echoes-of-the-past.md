# Chapter Outline: Echoes of the Past

## Chapter Purpose
This chapter aims to deepen the mystery surrounding the bone disc, the Pryzi key, and the token, while introducing a tangible threat from Vishara’s influence. It will push the party to confront uncomfortable truths about Jarl Mathr and begin to unravel the connection between the Shadow-Walkers, the Lösweg tradition, and the ancient history of Karsac. Ultimately, the chapter will force the party to make a difficult choice regarding Pell Duvash and the information he holds.

## Starting State
The party is currently in Karsac, possessing the original bone disc, the Pryzi manifest, and having recently discovered the token belongs to Jarl Mathr. Korvann is present and will react strongly to the original bone disc. Two Shadow-Walkers carrying the Pryzi vault key have departed Halvash. Erwing Smallfoot is en route with a coded message for Serris.

## Player Knowledge
knownFacts is 0 – The players know nothing confirmed yet. They have observed events and heard rumors, but nothing has been definitively proven.

## DM Truth
*   Erik is being held by Vane, who is attempting to extract information about the party's investigation. Vane believes the party is getting too close to Mathr.
*   The coded message from the Cobalt Monk is a warning about Mathr's involvement with Vishara, and a plea to find and protect Pell Duvash.
*   Pell Duvash has a complete, unredacted copy of the Pryzi manifest and knows the location of the Pryzi vault. He is terrified of Vane and Mathr.
*   Korvann’s reaction to the bone disc will trigger a fragmented memory of his own past, revealing a connection to the Shadow-Walkers and a lost artifact.
*   Rowan's Crow is subtly guiding him towards a specific location within Karsac - the ruins of an ancient Khôràna temple.

## Core Pressure
The escalating threat of Vishara’s influence, the urgent need to understand the Pryzi key and manifest, and the growing suspicion surrounding Jarl Mathr all create immense pressure on the party. The arrival of Erwing and the potential appearance of Pell Duvash add further urgency.

## Active Factions and NPCs
*   **Vishara:** Wants to consolidate power and influence within Karsac, using Mathr as a puppet. Knows the party is investigating, and is subtly manipulating events to discredit them. Will use Vane to deal with threats.
*   **Jarl Mathr:** Wants to maintain his position and power, concealing his involvement with Vishara. Knows the party is investigating, and is using Vane to monitor them. Will attempt to manipulate the party and discredit them.
*   **Vane:** Mathr's loyal servant, acting as his enforcer. Knows the party is investigating, and will attempt to silence them.
*   **Korvann:** Wants to understand the bone disc markings and their connection to his past. Knows he is following corrupted copies, and is unaware of the original's power.
*   **Pell Duvash:** Wants to survive and reveal the truth about the Pryzi vault. Knows Vane wants him dead, and is awaiting a chance to escape.
*   **The Buyer:** Wants the artifacts the party is uncovering. Knows the party possesses valuable information and will attempt to manipulate them.

## Scene Spine
1.  **Purpose:** Introduce Erwing and the coded message. **Location:** Karsac marketplace. **Pressure:** Decode the message while avoiding Vane’s scrutiny. **Choices:** Attempt to decode it themselves, seek Serris’s help, or ignore it. **Clues:** The message itself. **Failure:** Vane intercepts Erwing, delaying the message.
2.  **Purpose:** Korvann’s reaction to the bone disc. **Location:** Party’s lodging. **Pressure:** Manage Korvann’s emotional and potentially dangerous reaction. **Choices:** Attempt to calm him, study him, or leave him alone. **Clues:** Fragmented memories from Korvann, hinting at a lost artifact and the Shadow-Walkers. **Failure:** Korvann’s reaction attracts unwanted attention.
3.  **Purpose:** Locate and interact with Pell Duvash. **Location:** Torweg’s upper market. **Pressure:** Extract information from Duvash while avoiding Vane’s assassins. **Choices:** Approach him openly, attempt to bribe him, or track him discreetly. **Clues:** Duvash’s fear, his cryptic warnings, and the location of the Pryzi vault. **Failure:** Duvash is captured or killed by Vane’s agents.
4.  **Purpose:** Investigate the Pryzi vault location. **Location:** A remote location outside Karsac. **Pressure:** Navigate the terrain and potential traps guarding the vault. **Choices:** Force entry, search for a hidden entrance, or attempt to decipher clues. **Clues:** Ancient runes, a hidden mechanism. **Failure:** Trigger a trap, alerting Vane to their actions.
5.  **Purpose:** Confrontation with Vane. **Location:** The Pryzi vault location or a pursuit through Karsac. **Pressure:** Escape Vane’s pursuit and potentially confront him directly. **Choices:** Fight, flee, or attempt to negotiate. **Clues:** Vane’s motivations, Mathr’s involvement. **Failure:** Capture by Vane, leading to interrogation.

## Optional Scenes
*   **Rowan’s Vision:** Rowan experiences a vivid vision of the ancient Khôràna temple, triggered by proximity to a specific location in Karsac.
*   **Serris’s Archive:** The party can return to Serris’s archive to research the bone disc markings or the Pryzi vault.
*   **The Buyer’s Contact:** The Buyer attempts to contact the party, offering assistance or information in exchange for a favor.

## Clues and Reveals
*   **Player-Safe Clues:** Erwing’s coded message, Korvann’s fragmented memories, Pell Duvash’s warnings, the location of the Pryzi vault.
*   **Deeper Implications:** The bone disc markings are connected to a lost Khôràna civilization. Mathr is being manipulated by Vishara. The Pryzi vault contains a powerful artifact.
*   **DM-Only Truth:** Erik is being held by Vane. The Cobalt Monk’s message was a desperate plea for help.

## Adversaries and Obstacles
*   **Social:** Vane's manipulation and intimidation tactics.
*   **Procedural:** Deciphering the coded message, locating Pell Duvash, navigating the terrain to the Pryzi vault.
*   **Factional:** Avoiding detection by Vane’s agents, dealing with the Buyer’s influence.
*   **Investigative:** Unraveling the connection between the Shadow-Walkers, the Lösweg tradition, and the ancient history of Karsac.
*   **Combat:** Potential confrontations with Vane and his agents. (Refer to Vishara’s agents corpus for combat abilities).

## Fail-Forward Paths
*   **Failure to decode the message:** The party misses a crucial warning about Mathr’s involvement with Vishara.
*   **Failure to locate Pell Duvash:** The party loses a valuable source of information about the Pryzi vault.
*   **Capture by Vane:** The party is interrogated, potentially revealing their investigation and compromising their position.
*   **Ignoring Rowan’s vision:** The party misses a vital clue about the history of Karsac and the significance of the bone disc.

## End Conditions
*   **Success:** The party deciphers the coded message, locates and secures the Pryzi vault, and uncovers evidence of Mathr’s involvement with Vishara.
*   **Partial:** The party discovers some information about the Pryzi vault and Mathr’s involvement, but faces setbacks and loses valuable allies.
*   **Failure:** The party is captured by Vane, their investigation is compromised, and Mathr’s influence over Karsac strengthens.
