# The Waking

**Canon File ID:** `forces/the-waking`
**Retrieval Summary:** The current rising of Maharuq's pressure; began three months ago when the Stormwatch vault opened itself; not caused by any agent — the agents are responding to it.

> The current rising of Maharuq's pressure; began three months ago when the Stormwatch vault opened itself; the agents of Maharuq are not causing it — they are responding to it.

## Overview

The Waking is the name given to the current period of increasing pressure from Maharuq's concentrated presence in the substrate of Karsac. It is not a sudden event. It is a gradual intensification that crossed a threshold three months before the campaign's Chapter 2 events, when the sealed location in the Stormwatch Mountains above Törweg opened itself — not forced, but because the pressure from below had increased to the point where the seal could no longer hold.

## Description

### What It Is

Maharuq does not sleep in the way living things sleep. What the oral traditions call sleeping and waking are the difference between a pressure that is stable and a pressure that is increasing. The Waking is the latter — a process underway, not a moment, not an act.

The distinction that the Sourcebook's author considers most important for how the players understand their situation: the Waking is not being caused by the Captains, the Yantravaq, or anyone acting on Maharuq's behalf. It is the other way around. The waking is occurring, and the agents of Maharuq are responding to it — trying to position themselves advantageously relative to a process that would be happening whether or not they existed.

The players are not trying to prevent something that is being caused. They are trying to shape the conditions of something happening regardless.

### The Threshold Event

The Waking's crossing of its most recent threshold: the sealed location in the Stormwatch Mountains opened itself two days before the autumn equinox of the current year — three months before the Chapter 2 events. Twelve objects were recovered from inside. Aldric Vane began moving them south on Jarl Mathr's orders without questioning why. See `events/stormwatch-vault-opening` and `items/the-twelve-artefacts`.

### Observable Effects

The Waking expresses at multiple scales simultaneously:

- **Continental:** Maharuq's pressure in the substrate of Karsac is measurably increasing. The Hiding's mechanisms are under stress. The Captains are repositioning.
- **Regional:** Törweg is the most-worked town in Lösweg — the Thinning is measurable there in ways it isn't yet in Valweg. Sound carries strangely. The number of people is wrong.
- **Local:** The Maw above Törweg has had something taken from it. The geometry in the fracture corresponds to the bone disc. Something was left unfinished.
- **Individual:** The Carver carves the same spiral every afternoon and does not know why. Brix's description of Tor Ashfen — "asking different questions" — is the Waking's influence at the individual scale.

## Player Knowledge

Players in Chapter 2 can observe the Waking's effects without naming it:

- Törweg feels wrong before they have language for why.
- The Maw investigation (Thread B) produces the Aeorian fragment and the Waking section — the first time the players can name what they are inside.
- The bone disc geometry matching the Maw carving is the player-facing evidence that Maharuq's oldest mark and the current events are connected.

The Waking as a named concept — and the crucial fact that no one is causing it — arrives via the Sourcebook's Part IV §VII, suggested discovery in Törweg Thread B.

## DM-Only

### What the Discovery Delivers

When players find the Part IV §VII fragment at the Maw, they should understand:

1. The Waking is not the enemy's plan. It is the ground the plan runs on.
2. Stopping Vishara does not stop the Waking. It shapes the conditions of a world where the Waking is happening.
3. This means victory is possible. Not complete victory — the Waking continues. But real victory: shaping the conditions of what the world wakes into.

This is the reframe that changes the players' understanding of their mission. They are not trying to plug a volcano. They are trying to ensure that when the volcano erupts, the right people are standing in the right place.

### The Long View

The Waking has been building since before the current campaign. The sealed location's opening was the most recent threshold, but the pressure has been increasing for longer than sixty years — longer than Mathr's shaping. The Captains' preparation spans centuries. The Waking is the frame inside which all of this is happening.

## Ambiguities

- The precise timeline of when the Waking's current acceleration began (as opposed to the specific threshold event of the vault opening) is not specified in sources. Three months ago marks the vault; the broader intensification has no start date in the corpus.
