# Brynja's Hall, Törweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/torweg/brynjas-hall`
**Retrieval Summary:** Brynja's hall; central fire pit, dark compass-rose banners, empty Housecarl-Captain's seat; site of the briefing and the four threads.

> Brynja's hall; central fire pit; dark compass-rose banners; the empty Housecarl-Captain's seat; site of the briefing.

## Overview

A long timbered building set into the cliff face above the western dock — two storeys, fire already laid when the company arrives. This is where Brynja takes the company after the dock. The briefing happens here. The four threads are named here. And, on the happy path, the hearthstone is lifted and the artefact placed on the table here.

## Description

The hall has the look of a space that has been used by council for years: dark timber, compass-rose banners, a long table, the fire pit at the centre. The Housecarl-Captain's seat at the head of the table is empty. It has been effectively empty for two years — Vane holds the title but Brynja has been holding the city.

Brynja offers food. Most of the company will not eat it. She does not press.

She tells them what she has been keeping records of and watches their faces for the moment they understand what she is describing.

Then she gives them four threads. She does not call them threads. She says she has work to do and that she will be at the council house by midday if they need her.

### The Hearthstone

Behind a stone in the hearth: one of the twelve artefacts. Brynja placed it there because she felt it should not leave. She does not know what it is. On the happy path, at the session's close, she removes the stone herself and places it on the table. She does not explain. She does not need to.

### What She Says (Happy Path)

Aldric Vane has been moving cargo south to Bruithwr for eight weeks on Mathr's orders. He does not seem to know what he is doing. That is what frightens her. Floki's captain came back from Halvash with questions; Vane's men took him a fortnight ago. She has one of the artefacts hidden. She does not know what it is. She knows it should not leave. And: *"There is a name I do not say in this hall. Not yet."*

## DM-Only

The name she does not say is Mathr. She will write it on paper at the session's close and tell them not to open it until they are on the Greyback. The name she will not say aloud is the thing the Truthspeaker has warned her against — not explicitly, but she has heard the warning in his presence on the dock.

The artefact reacts when the recovered cargo is brought near it. A pulse of warmth, felt only by Brynja when she returns to her hearth. Enough.
