# The White Tower

**Canon File ID:** `places/white-tower`
**Retrieval Summary:** A religious order or institution whose light was older than the continent; resistant to description; remembered by older powers under earlier names; Chapter 5 forthcoming.

> A religious order or institution of unusual age and resistance to description; its light was older than the continent.

## Overview

The White Tower has resisted ordinary description longer than any institution in this volume. Its scholars are few, its public statements rarer, and its reputation across Karsac varies by region between reverence, suspicion, and the kind of unease that attaches to powers that have been useful precisely as long as they have been quiet.

## Description

The Sourcebook's Chapter 5 is marked *forthcoming* with outline sections only. What is currently known:

- The White Tower is an order or institution of some kind — whether primarily religious, scholarly, or something else is not resolved in available sources.
- It is old enough to have been remembered by older powers under earlier names.
- The Sourcebook's chapter outline explicitly includes a section titled "Caution — what we have chosen not to write down." This is the only sourcebook chapter to include such a heading.
