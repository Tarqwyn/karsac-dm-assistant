# The Meridian

**Canon File ID:** `places/the-meridian`
**Retrieval Summary:** Halvash inn; D'rek's; the company's base after the alley fight; the night that closed the west.

> The Halvash inn; D'rek's; the company's last ground before the Whale-Road.

## Overview

The Meridian is the inn at Halvash where the company spent the night between the alley fight and the Whale-Road crossing. D'rek has kept it for twenty years. She knows — the way an innkeeper of twenty years knows — when people are not done with the night.

## Description

After the fight ended in the small hours, the company was at the Meridian. D'rek poured drinks. She put bread and salt on the table. She put one extra chair near the hearth. She did not ask what happened.

The hearth was low. The bone disc was in someone's pocket and still warm. The sealed document Rowan lifted from the boy was unopened on the table. Nobody touched it.

This is where Edvar came to the door. This is where Floki sat down an hour before first light and told them about his grandmother. This is where the company decided — by staying, rather than going to the Windmere — that the west was not the road.

By dawn the Windmere was on the horizon, sails up, headed home. The west closed while they were learning what was happening to the east.
