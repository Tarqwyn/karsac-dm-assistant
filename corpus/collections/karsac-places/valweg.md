## Overview

Valweg is nestled deep within the fjords of the Bay of Whales — the seat of Lösweg governance, such as the concept applies in Lösweg. It is a city of dark timber, fire-lit mead halls, and the constant sound of water, creak of rope, and cry of seabirds. To stand in Valweg at dawn, with fog rising off the fjord and longships moored in silhouette, is to feel one has stepped not merely into another land but into an earlier age of the world.

## Description

Valweg sits more protected than Törweg — deeper in the fjords, further from the open Whale-Road, harder to reach. Where Törweg is the harder, more exposed of the two cities, Valweg is the political centre: seat of the inner council, home of Jarl Mathr's formal authority, and the destination of King Dugweb's annual visit.

The inner council sits in Valweg. Jarl Beorn leads it; Jarl Mathr sits on it and has shaped it for sixty years. The annual visit from King Dugweb is managed from here — Mathr has handled every visit for sixty years, keeping the council, keeping the city, keeping a carefully constructed silence between Dugweb and the country he has come to see.

A specific note from the Ch 2 BETA: the tapestries in Valweg's council chamber — the old ones that recorded specific battles, specific names, specific defeats as well as victories — have been burned. The new ones are beautiful and general. This is Vishara's work at the civic-memory scale.

### Maret

The archivist Maret, referenced in the Sourcebook's Part IV section VI, directs those investigating Mathr's genealogical lineage toward the Valweg archive. Maret is a Chapter 3 contact. See `npcs/maret`.

## Atmosphere & Themes

Valweg is where the trail leads after Törweg. It is the more defended place — deeper in the fjords, protected by water and approach time — and yet, by the Chapter 2 storyarch, it is also losing ground. The tapestries were burned. The community where argument has ended is a community where something has been lost. Valweg is ahead of Törweg in one sense and behind in another: more politically managed, more thoroughly smoothed.

## Player Knowledge

Players learn of Valweg primarily through Brynja's briefing: Jarl Beorn is there, leads the council, has a deep relationship with King Dugweb, and is deceived rather than corrupted. Brynja's recommendation is to reach Valweg before Dugweb's annual visit and put the truth in front of the right man. The road north is not safe.

## DM-Only

Mathr's presence in Valweg is the political centre of Vishara's campaign-1 operation. Session 4 — the unmasking — takes place here. The archive here is where the council entry and paradox section of the Yantravaq appendix can be discovered (Chapter 3).

The Truthspeaker operates from Valweg — he was sent from Valweg when Mathr's intelligence confirmed the Greyback was coming back with strangers. His arrival in Törweg the night before the company's arrival is not coincidental.

## Ambiguities

- The specific geography of Valweg relative to Törweg (sailing time, road distance) is not specified in the Sourcebook's Chapter 2 §I beyond "deeper in the fjords." The Chapter 2 BETA references sailing along the coast for the Bay of Whales as the next leg.
