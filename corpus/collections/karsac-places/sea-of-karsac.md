# The Sea of Karsac

**Canon File ID:** `places/sea-of-karsac`

> The western coastal waters of Karsac; the sea-road connecting Halvash and Lösweg.

## Overview

The Sea of Karsac is the western coastal waters of the continent — the stretch of sea between Halvash and the Lösweg coastline navigated by the Greyback in Chapter 2. It appears in the Sourcebook Index under Places but receives no dedicated section; it is a geographic constant on the western approach.

## Description

The Sea of Karsac is the Whale-Road of the campaign's Chapter 2 crossing — two days from Halvash to Törweg aboard the Greyback. Its waters are cold, the crossing subject to the weather patterns that shape Lösweg culture. The Bay of Whales opens from the Sea of Karsac into the Lösweg fjords.

No further detail is available in the current sourcebook beyond the map reference.
