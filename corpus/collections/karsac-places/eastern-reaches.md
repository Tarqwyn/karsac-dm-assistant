# The Eastern Reaches

**Canon File ID:** `places/eastern-reaches`
**Retrieval Summary:** A sequence of peoples, customs, and roads extending east from Iambarsir; includes Barbytura and the Yngondi Wastes; treated with varying degrees of Vushhiri confidence.

> A sequence of peoples, customs, and roads that begin at the eastern markets of Iambarsir and extend, by routes and rumours, well past the limits of any chart Vushhir has been able to verify.

## Overview

The Eastern Reaches are not a single polity. They are a sequence of peoples, customs, and roads — beginning at Iambarsir and extending past the limits of honest Vushhiri cartography. The Sourcebook's forthcoming Chapter 6 acknowledges up front the methodological problem that almost everything known of these peoples comes either from their own remarkable hospitality (Barbytura) or from traders whose interest in accuracy is professionally limited.

## Description

The Sourcebook's Chapter 6 is marked *forthcoming* with outline sections only. What is currently known:

- **Barbytura** — the great riding houses east of Iambarsir. A culture of extraordinary oral generosity; the difficulty lies not in getting them to speak but in determining where history ends and poetry begins — and whether the distinction matters to them. Treated separately in `places/barbytura`.
- **The Long Roads** — caravans, way-stations, and the political economy of distance. Eastern metals, felt, lacquer, bone carvings, and things from empires that simplify their names when passing through Vushhiri records.
- **The Yngondi Wastes** — treated with reluctance and incompleteness even in the Sourcebook. Vushhir maintains records, warnings, silences, and sealed files. Treated separately in `places/yngondi-wastes`.
- **The Further East** — beyond verified charting. Vushhir has heard things, verified some, and declined to repeat others.
