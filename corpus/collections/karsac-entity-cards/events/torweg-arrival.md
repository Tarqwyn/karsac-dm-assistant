# The Törweg Arrival

**Canon File ID:** `events/torweg-arrival`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/events/torweg-arrival`

**Entity Type:** Event — Chapter 2 session opener

**Retrieval Summary:** Dawn of day three. The Greyback enters Törweg's deep bay. Three figures on the wharf where there should be twenty. The flag at half-mast — nobody knows for whom. Voices carrying a little too far. Brynja stands waiting at the near end; the man with the black pin stands at the far end. Floki goes still at the foredeck. Brynja's first line to Floki: "Floki. You came back with strangers. Why."

**Use For:** Identifying the Törweg Arrival as the Chapter 2 session opening event, routing to the wharf scene, and distinguishing it from Brynja's Briefing (the subsequent scene in her hall).
