# The Whale-Road Crossing

**Canon File ID:** `events/whale-road-crossing`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/events/whale-road-crossing`

**Entity Type:** Event — Chapter 2 transit

**Retrieval Summary:** The two-day crossing aboard the Greyback from Halvash to Törweg. Key beats: the cargo is unveiled to the company; Floki gives Ragnfrid his grandmother's arm-ring; the Sea Hag encounter (Floki's father's clothing on a thrall); the Deepwhale registers the cargo and does not attack; arrival at dawn with three figures on the wharf.

**Use For:** Identifying the Whale-Road Crossing as the Chapter 2 transit event and the arm-ring, Sea Hag, and Deepwhale beats.

**Primary Detail File:** `events/whale-road-crossing`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Törweg Arrival (the subsequent event when the Greyback enters the harbour; distinct event).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Sea Hag — encounter drawn by the cargo.
- Ragnfrid's Arm-Ring — given by Floki during the crossing.
- The Deepwhale — registers; does not attack.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe.
