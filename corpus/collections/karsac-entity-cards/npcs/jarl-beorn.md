# Jarl Beorn

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/jarl-beorn`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/jarl-beorn`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** Jarl Beorn leads the Lösweg inner council at Valweg and has a deep personal relationship with King Dugweb built across decades of annual visits. He has trusted Mathr for sixty years with no reason not to. He is deceived, not shaped — his convictions are his own. Brynja identifies him as the route forward: if shown the evidence, he will act, and acting means taking it to Dugweb.

**Use For:** Identifying Jarl Beorn as the happy-path council contact and Brynja's named route north, distinguishing him sharply from Jarl Mathr.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/jarl-beorn`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Jarl Mathr (sits on the same council; shaped by Vishara; the antagonist — not Beorn), House Mathr (the noble house; not Beorn), King Dugweb (Zörsdkog's king; Beorn's ally; distinct entity), Aldric Vane (the Housecarl-Captain in Törweg; not Beorn).

**Key Relationships:**
- Jarl Mathr — they sit on the same council; Beorn has been deceived by him for sixty years.
- King Dugweb — deep personal relationship; Beorn is the route to getting Dugweb the truth.
- Brynja — she names Beorn as the route forward from Törweg.
