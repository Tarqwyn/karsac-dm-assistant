# Cumbria

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/cumbria`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/cumbria`

**Entity Type:** NPC — supporting

**Retrieval Summary:** Cumbria is the Halvashi pilot who navigates the Greyback from Halvash to Törweg across the Whale-Road. She speaks almost no Common. She reads water colour as though it were script. She is a practical presence — her function is to deliver the company and then leave. She adjusts course twice on the first morning for reasons no one aboard can see, and no one asks.

**Use For:** Identifying Cumbria as the Whale-Road pilot, routing to the crossing event.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/cumbria`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Floki (the Greyback's crew chief; distinct NPC), Sygna (the translator; distinct NPC).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Greyback — she pilots it for the crossing.
- Whale-Road Crossing — the event she navigates.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe.
