# The Carver

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/the-carver`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/the-carver`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** The Carver is an unnamed male worker at the Cooperage in Törweg's upper market. His brother Hjalmar went into the Maw three months ago and came out wrong. Since then the Carver has produced the same spiral on scraps of wood every afternoon, compulsively, without knowing why. The spiral is structurally identical to a fragment of the bone disc. DM-only: he is a Vishara symptom — the pattern propagates through him without his awareness.

**Use For:** Identifying the Carver as the Thread B cooperage NPC and Vishara-symptom character, distinguishing him from Brynja, the Truthspeaker, and Threshold-Bound Echo.
