# Floki

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/floki`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/floki`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** Floki is the crew chief of the Greyback — not young, not old, a Whale-Road sailor his whole life. His grandmother was a sea-reader who gave him a two-part prophecy when he was twelve. The first half came true the night the Shadow-Walkers came to Halvash. He holds the seventh artefact — the pierced river-stone — apart from the others in his coat and has told no one. He speaks almost no Common; Sygna translates. He vouches for the company to Brynja.

**Use For:** Identifying Floki as the Greyback's crew chief and prophecy-carrier, routing to the pierced river-stone and the whale-road crossing, distinguishing from Sygna (the translator).

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/floki`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Sygna (Floki's translator and crewmate; a distinct NPC), Tor Ashfen (captain of the Coldfront; missing; not the Greyback).

**Key Relationships:**
- The pierced river-stone — the seventh object, kept separate in his coat; DM-only.
- Brynja — has known her twenty years; his vouching opens her door.
- Sygna — his translator; reads him better than most people read their own family.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The second half of the grandmother's prophecy is DM-only.
