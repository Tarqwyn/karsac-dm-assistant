# The Threshold-Bound Echo

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/threshold-bound-echo`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/threshold-bound-echo`

**Entity Type:** NPC — encounter / guardian

**Retrieval Summary:** The Threshold-Bound Echo guards the lower shaft of the Maw fracture above Törweg. CR 4 large aberration, neutral territorial. It tests, withdraws, and tests again. It does not pursue beyond the fracture. It carries a dry, mineral smell — the same smell the Shadow-Walkers carried in the Halvash alley. Connected to Hjalmar's fate: the Carver's brother went into the Maw and what came out became this.

**Use For:** Identifying the Threshold-Bound Echo as the Thread B Maw encounter, distinguishing it from The Carver and from Hjalmar.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/threshold-bound-echo`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Carver (the cooperage NPC; the Echo's brother went into the Maw; the Carver is the one who stayed outside), Hjalmar (the Carver's brother whose fate connects to the Echo; a distinct figure), The Sea Hag (the Whale-Road encounter; distinct).

**Key Relationships:**
- Hjalmar — went into the Maw and came out wrong; the Echo's connection.
- The Carver — Hjalmar's brother; sends Ragnfrid to the Cooperage, not the Maw directly.
- The Maw — the fracture it guards.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only. Players encounter it but its connection to Hjalmar is DM-layer.
