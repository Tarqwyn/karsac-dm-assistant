# Edvar Solm

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/edvar-solm`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/edvar-solm`

**Entity Type:** NPC — supporting

**Retrieval Summary:** Edvar Solm has been Halvash harbourmaster for nineteen years. He does not leave — his place is the threshold, not the road. He gave the company a working Lösweg vocabulary and a letter of introduction before they sailed. The night after the alley fight he came to the Meridian and said: "I have been the harbourmaster of Halvash for nineteen years. I have never had to think about the Yngondi Wastes. I am asking you to find out what is wrong in Lösweg." Then he went back to his post.

**Use For:** Identifying Edvar Solm as the Halvash threshold-keeper and the company's mission-giver, distinguishing him from Serris.
