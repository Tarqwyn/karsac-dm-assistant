# The Sea Hag of the Whale-Road

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/the-sea-hag`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/the-sea-hag`

**Entity Type:** NPC — encounter

**Retrieval Summary:** The Sea Hag is a Whale-Road encounter creature drawn by the artefact cargo the Greyback carries. She arrives with fog, a voice carrying too far across open ocean, and three Drowned Thralls — one in old Lösweg clothing in patterns Floki's father wore. She withdraws when the Deepwhale rises beneath the ship: not from defeat but from perceiving what the cargo is and choosing to disengage.

**Use For:** Identifying the Sea Hag as the Whale-Road crossing encounter, routing to the whale-road-crossing event.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/the-sea-hag`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Deepwhale (which causes her withdrawal; a distinct presence), Threshold-Bound Echo (the Maw encounter entity; distinct).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Twelve Artefacts — the cargo that drew her.
- Floki — one thrall wears his father's clothing; he says nothing during combat.
- The Deepwhale — rises to intervene; she disengages.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only. Players encounter her but the full framing is DM-layer.
