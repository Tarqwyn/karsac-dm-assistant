# Pell Duvash

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/pell-duvash`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/pell-duvash`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** Pell Duvash is a mid-fifties Törweg trading-house merchant who served as intermediary for three consignments of artefacts moving south to Bruithwr. He knew what he was handling by the end. A note left by Erik Mathr told him to stay and tell the truth to whoever came. He has been waiting. The assassins who arrive mid-testimony confirm someone wants the conversation stopped. He names Captain Kohlmar in Bruithwr as the recipient.

**Use For:** Identifying Pell Duvash as the Thread C merchant and testimony NPC, routing to the duvash-attack event and the Kohlmar lead.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/pell-duvash`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Kohlmar (the Bruithwr captain who collected the artefacts; not Duvash), Aldric Vane (the Housecarl-Captain who issued the letters; not Duvash), Erik Mathr (who left Duvash the note; not Duvash himself).

**Key Relationships:**
- Erik Mathr — left the note that keeps Duvash in Törweg and willing to testify.
- Aldric's Letter — the third letter had a different handwriting in the body; Duvash noticed.
- Kohlmar — the Bruithwr captain collecting the artefacts; named by Duvash.
