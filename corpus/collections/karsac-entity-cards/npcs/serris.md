# Serris

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/serris`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/npcs/serris`

**Entity Type:** NPC — major

**Retrieval Summary:** Serris is a Cobalt Soul archivist from the Halvash archive who asked to travel with the company and was accepted. They are the closest any Cobalt Soul scholar has come to connecting the fragments the Hiding has left in Exandrian consciousness. On the Greyback, they noticed the geometry of the cargo padding impressions before anyone else. In the Maw, they recognised the connection between the Aeorian note and the scroll case margin — the same hand, after two thousand years — and went very quiet.

**Use For:** Identifying Serris as the Cobalt Soul scholar-companion and pattern-recognition NPC, routing to the Hiding's scholarly context.

**Primary Detail File:** `npcs/serris`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Edvar Solm (the Halvash harbourmaster; stayed at the threshold; not Serris).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Aeorian note — Serris recognises it as the same hand as the scroll case margin.
- The Hiding — the cosmological force Serris has been building toward understanding.
- Erwing — delivers the coded message to Serris; Serris goes quiet reading it.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe.
