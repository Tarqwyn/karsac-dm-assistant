# The Twelve Artefacts

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-twelve-artefacts`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-twelve-artefacts`

**Entity Type:** Item — artefact set

**Retrieval Summary:** The twelve artefacts are the components of the structure that holds Vishara to material existence on this plane. They were recovered from the Maw three months before Chapter 2 when the sealed vault opened. Seven were diverted toward Halvash; five remain in and around Törweg. Vane is moving them south on Mathr's orders. The artefacts are not weapons — they are components of a binding.

**Use For:** Identifying the twelve artefacts as a set and their cosmological function, routing to individual artefact files, and distinguishing the set from specific components.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-twelve-artefacts`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Brynja's Hidden Artefact (one specific artefact of the twelve, held by Brynja; file at items/brynjas-hidden-artefact), The Pierced River-Stone (the seventh object, held by Floki; DM-only), The Bone Disc (Vishara's symbol; recovered from the alley fight; not one of the twelve), The Pryzi Key (recovered from the alley; not one of the twelve), The Maw Carving (the wall pattern; a handout; not itself one of the artefacts), The Spiral Carver's Whittlings (the cooperage NPC's output; not an artefact).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Maw — the vault they were recovered from.
- Aldric Vane — moving five of them south on Mathr's orders.
- Floki — has the seventh (the pierced river-stone) separately in his coat.
- Brynja — holds one behind a stone in her hearth.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The cosmological function is DM-layer. Players know twelve objects exist and are being moved.
