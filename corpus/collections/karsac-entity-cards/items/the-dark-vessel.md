# The Dark Vessel

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-dark-vessel`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/the-dark-vessel`

**Entity Type:** Item — PC artefact (growing item)

**Retrieval Summary:** The Dark Vessel is Xyrrathh's Ashvein container — the same rare glass-metal as the Shadow-Walkers' spikes and the Truthspeaker's pin. It holds acid suspending a black shifting substance. Its connection to Maharuq is established; whether it is an instrument of dissolution or a captured fragment is deliberately left open. The Threshold-Bound Echo treated Xyrrathh differently.

**Use For:** Identifying the Dark Vessel as Xyrrathh's growing artefact and the Ashvein connection for Xyrrathh.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/the-dark-vessel`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Ashvein (the material the vessel is made of; distinct from the vessel as object), The Bone Disc (Vishara's symbol; distinct item).

**Key Relationships:**
- Xyrrathh — the PC who carries it.
- Ashvein — the material it is made of; connects to the Shadow-Walker supply chain.
- Maharuq — the force it is linked to.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. Full cosmological framing is DM-layer.
