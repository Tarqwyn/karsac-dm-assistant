# Aldric's Letter of Authority

**Canon File ID:** `items/aldric-letter`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/aldric-letter`

**Entity Type:** Item — document / handout (H16)

**Retrieval Summary:** The most recent letter of authority given to Aldric Vane. Mathr's seal is correct; the signature is correct; the body text is in a different hand. Duvash noticed. Vane did not. The letter names Captain Kohlmar in Bruithwr as the recipient of the artefacts. Recoverable from Vane's body or from the south dock warehouse.

**Use For:** Identifying Aldric's Letter as the document that proves a third party is now writing Mathr's orders, and as the lead to Kohlmar in Bruithwr.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/aldric-letter`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Mathr's Token (the bronze collar token; a different item of evidence), Brynja's Ledger (Brynja's cargo record; a different document).

**Key Relationships:**
- Aldric Vane — given to him; he has not examined the handwriting.
- Jarl Mathr — whose seal and signature appear on it; but the body is not his hand.
- Kohlmar — named as recipient in Bruithwr; the forward lead.
- Pell Duvash — the third letter is the one he noticed was different.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The handwriting analysis is player-accessible; the identity of the third writer is DM-layer.
