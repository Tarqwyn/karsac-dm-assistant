# Brynja's Ledger (Cargo Record)

**Canon File ID:** `items/brynjas-ledger`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/brynjas-ledger`

**Entity Type:** Item — document / handout (H5)

**Retrieval Summary:** Brynja's private record kept over two years — dates, vessel descriptions, rough cargo weights. She shows it to the company in her hall during the briefing. Handout H5, posted when Brynja shares the record.

**Use For:** Identifying Brynja's Ledger as the cargo record handout, distinguishing it from Brynja's Hidden Artefact and from Aldric's Letter.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/brynjas-ledger`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Brynja's Hidden Artefact (the artefact behind the hearthstone; a physical object, not a document), Aldric's Letter (Vane's letter of authority; a different document).

**Key Relationships:**
- Brynja Thorgrimsdotter — she kept it; she shows it; on the happy path she gives it to the company.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe once shown.
