# Brynja's Hidden Artefact

**Canon File ID:** `items/brynjas-hidden-artefact`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/items/brynjas-hidden-artefact`

**Entity Type:** Item — artefact (one of twelve)

**Retrieval Summary:** One of the twelve artefacts from the Stormwatch vault, kept by Brynja Thorgrimsdotter behind a stone in her hearth. She does not know what it is. She knows it should not leave — a knowing that arrived the day the objects came down from the Maw. It produced a pulse of warmth felt at her hearth at the session's close when the recovered artefacts were near the south dock.

**Use For:** Identifying this specific artefact as the one Brynja holds, distinguishing it from the set of twelve and from other individual artefacts.

**Primary Detail File:** `items/brynjas-hidden-artefact`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Twelve Artefacts (the full set; this is one of them), The Pierced River-Stone (Floki's artefact; seventh object; distinct), The Bone Disc (Vishara's symbol; not one of the twelve; not an artefact in this sense), Brynja's Ledger (her cargo record; a document, not an artefact).

**Key Relationships:**
- Brynja — holds it; does not know what it is; has told no one.
- The Twelve Artefacts — this is number unknown of twelve; part of the binding set.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. Brynja's knowledge of the artefact is player-accessible; the pulse reaction detail is DM-layer.
