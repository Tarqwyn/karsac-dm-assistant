# Vushhir

**Canon File ID:** `places/vushhir`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/vushhir`

**Entity Type:** Place — region / scholarly centre

**Retrieval Summary:** Vushhir is the civic-scholarly heart of Karsac — a civilisation that rose around inquiry rather than bloodline or conquest. Home to the Great Library of Vushhir and a Cobalt Soul presence. Serris trained there. The Sourcebook's scholarly voice (Maresh ul-Venn, Archivist Solenne vel Dravik) originates here.

**Use For:** Identifying Vushhir as the scholarly heartland and Great Library location.
