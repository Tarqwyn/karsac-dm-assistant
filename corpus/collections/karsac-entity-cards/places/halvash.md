# Halvash

**Canon File ID:** `places/halvash`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/halvash`

**Entity Type:** Place — port city

**Retrieval Summary:** Halvash is the anomalous port on Karsac's coast — the unique known point of regular contact between Karsac and Exandria. Home to a Cobalt Soul archive. The company spent Chapter 1 here; the alley fight (three Shadow-Walkers, the boy's death, the bone disc recovered) happened here. Edvar Solm is the harbourmaster. The company departed for the Whale-Road from Halvash.

**Use For:** Identifying Halvash as the Chapter 1 location and pre-Whale-Road base, routing to the alley fight event and Cobalt Soul archive, distinguishing it from Törweg.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/halvash`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Törweg (the Chapter 2 city), Bay of Whales (the sea north of the Whale-Road), The Hiding (the cosmological force; Halvash's anomalous status may be related to it).

**Key Relationships:**
- Serris — Cobalt Soul archivist; joined the company here.
- Edvar Solm — harbourmaster; asked the company to find out what is wrong in Lösweg.
- Shadow-Walkers — killed the boy in the alley here; the bone disc was recovered.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe. DM-layer questions about why Halvash is anomalous are not resolved in current canon.
