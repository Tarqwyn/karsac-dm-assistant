# Törweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/torweg`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/torweg`

**Entity Type:** Place — city

**Retrieval Summary:** Törweg is Lösweg's harder second city, built into the western cliff face above a single deep-bay harbour. It is the hub of Chapter 2. In current canon it shows three boats out instead of twenty, a flag at half-mast, and voices that carry too far — signs of the Thinning. The Stormwatch Mountains rise immediately to the east; the Maw is an hour up the foothill path. Sub-locations include the western dock, south dock, Saltbone Inn, Brynja's Hall, Pell Duvash's trading house, the Cooperage, and the council archives.

**Use For:** Identifying Törweg as the Chapter 2 setting, routing to its sub-location files, and distinguishing it from Valweg, Lösweg (the region), and the Maw.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/torweg`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Valweg (the seat of government, deeper in the fjords), Lösweg (the wider nation/region), The Maw (the fracture site in the foothills above Törweg), Stormwatch Mountains (the mountain range east of Törweg), Törweg sub-location files (torweg__*.md).

**Key Relationships:**
- Brynja Thorgrimsdotter — head of the Törweg council; the resistance voice Vishara has not reached.
- Aldric Vane — Housecarl-Captain, fifteen years; his authority derives from Jarl Mathr in Valweg.
- The Maw — one hour east up the Stormwatch foothill path.
- Valweg — the seat of Lösweg government; the destination of the road north.
