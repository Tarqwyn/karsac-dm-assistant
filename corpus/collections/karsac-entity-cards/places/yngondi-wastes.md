# The Yngondi Wastes

**Canon File ID:** `places/yngondi-wastes`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/yngondi-wastes`

**Entity Type:** Place — region (unmapped)

**Retrieval Summary:** The Yngondi Wastes are eastern unmapped territory in Karsac. Vushhir maintains records, warnings, silences, and several sealed files about them. They are the oldest stratum of Karsac oral tradition. The Captains are moving artefacts toward them. Korvann's settlement was destroyed to prevent access to something in this direction.

**Use For:** Identifying the Yngondi Wastes as the eastern sealed region and the Captains' destination.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/yngondi-wastes`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Eastern Reaches (the broader eastern region designation; the Wastes are within it), Stormwatch Mountains (the Lösweg mountain range; distinct location).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Captains — moving artefacts toward here.
- Korvann — his settlement was destroyed to prevent access.
