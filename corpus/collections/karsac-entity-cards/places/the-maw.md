# The Maw

**Canon File ID:** `places/the-maw`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/the-maw`

**Entity Type:** Place — natural feature / fracture site

**Retrieval Summary:** The Maw is the fracture in the Stormwatch foothills an hour's climb above Törweg, which opened itself three months before Chapter 2. Twelve objects were recovered from inside; Vane's housecarls missed the Aeorian note. The shaft walls are scored with the same geometry as the bone disc, continued past the disc's edge. The Threshold-Bound Echo guards the interior. Thread B investigation site.

**Use For:** Identifying the Maw as the artefact origin site, distinguishing it from the Maw carving (handout) and Törweg, and routing to Thread B investigation detail.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/the-maw`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Törweg (the city below; the Maw is above it, not in it), Stormwatch Mountains (the broader range; the Maw is in the foothills), The Maw Carving (handout item H13 — the extended bone disc pattern; distinct from the place itself), Threshold-Bound Echo (the entity guarding the interior), The Carver (the NPC in the Cooperage, not in the Maw).

**Key Relationships:**
- The bone disc — the Maw walls continue its geometry past where the disc ends.
- Threshold-Bound Echo — guards the lower shaft; Thread B combat encounter.
- The Carver — his brother Hjalmar entered the Maw; came out wrong.
- Twelve artefacts — recovered from here three months ago; five remain in or near Törweg.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The Aeorian note text and its cosmological implications are DM-layer.
