# Bay of Whales

**Canon File ID:** `places/bay-of-whales`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/bay-of-whales`

**Entity Type:** Place — sea / bay

**Retrieval Summary:** The Bay of Whales dominates Lösweg's northern coastline. In the most literal theological sense it is the body of one of the Lösweg gods — not a metaphor. The Whale-Road (the open crossing from Halvash) leads to its mouth; the fjords of Valweg and Törweg run off it inland. A Deepwhale acknowledged the Greyback on the Chapter 2 crossing.

**Use For:** Identifying the Bay of Whales as the northern sea of Lösweg, routing to Lösweg theological context, distinguishing from the Whale-Road and the Sea of Karsac.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/bay-of-whales`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Whale-Road (the open-sea crossing between Halvash and the Bay's mouth; distinct from the Bay itself), Sea of Karsac (the wider sea to the south).

**Key Relationships:**
- Valweg and Törweg — their fjords open from the Bay.
- Veldrasil and Ragnarholm — the Lösweg gods whose body the Bay is (in theological tradition).
