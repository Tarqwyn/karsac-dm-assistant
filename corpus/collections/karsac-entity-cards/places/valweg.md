# Valweg

**Canon File ID:** `places/valweg`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/valweg`

**Entity Type:** Place — city / seat of government

**Retrieval Summary:** Valweg is the seat of Lösweg governance, nestled deep in the Bay of Whales fjords — darker, more protected, and more politically central than Törweg. Jarl Beorn leads the inner council here; Jarl Mathr has sat on it and managed King Dugweb's annual visit for sixty years. The destination of the road north from Törweg. Archivist Maret is based here and is a Chapter 3 contact.

**Use For:** Identifying Valweg as the Lösweg political centre and Chapter 3 destination, distinguishing it from Törweg, and routing to inner council detail.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/valweg`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Törweg (the harder coastal city, Chapter 2 hub), Lösweg (the wider nation), Bay of Whales (the sea body the fjords open from).

**Key Relationships:**
- Jarl Beorn — leads the inner council here; Brynja's named route forward.
- Jarl Mathr — sits on the council; has shaped it for sixty years; the primary antagonist.
- King Dugweb — visits annually; Mathr manages the visit.
