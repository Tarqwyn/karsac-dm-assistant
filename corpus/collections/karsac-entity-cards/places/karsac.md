# Karsac

**Canon File ID:** `places/karsac`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/karsac`

**Entity Type:** Place — continent / campaign setting

**Retrieval Summary:** Karsac is the old continent in the northwest of Exandria, sealed from wider Exandrian awareness by Dhurvaq's Hiding. Its main regions include Lösweg (northwest, primary campaign area), Vushhir (civic-scholarly centre), and Zörsdkog (north). The continent title gives the campaign setting its name.

**Use For:** Identifying Karsac as the campaign continent, routing to its major regions.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/karsac`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Lösweg (the northwest nation; the primary campaign region within Karsac; distinct), Exandria (the wider world Karsac is part of but sealed from).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Hiding — the force sealing it from Exandrian awareness.
- Lösweg — the primary campaign region.
