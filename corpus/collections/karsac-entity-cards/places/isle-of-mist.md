# The Isle of Mist

**Canon File ID:** `places/isle-of-mist`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/places/isle-of-mist`

**Entity Type:** Place — island (mystery)

**Retrieval Summary:** The Isle of Mist is an island off the Lösweg coast, partially mapped, with fogs that defy charting. Legend holds the Old People — a lineage said to predate the current peoples of Lösweg — still live there. Vushhiri scholars who pressed the question were escorted to their lodgings.

**Use For:** Identifying the Isle of Mist as the Old People's legendary home and a Chapter 3 forward reference.

**Primary Detail File:** `places/isle-of-mist`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Bay of Whales (the body of water the Isle sits in; distinct), The Maw (the Stormwatch fracture site; entirely distinct place).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Old People — said to still live there.
- Bay of Whales — the sea it sits in.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The Old People's actual presence is not confirmed in current canon.
