# The Waking

**Canon File ID:** `forces/the-waking`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/the-waking`

**Entity Type:** Force — active condition

**Retrieval Summary:** The Waking is the current rising of Maharuq's pressure — the campaign's underlying cosmological urgency. It began three months ago when the Stormwatch vault opened itself. It was not caused by any agent; the agents (Vishara, the Captains) are responding to it. The vault opening, the artefact movements, and the Shadow-Walker escalation are all downstream of the Waking.

**Use For:** Identifying the Waking as the cosmological pressure driving Campaign 1 events, routing to Maharuq and the vault opening.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/the-waking`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Hiding (Dhurvaq's counter-arrangement; distinct), Maharuq (the primordial whose pressure the Waking is; distinct from the Waking as condition).

**Key Relationships:**
- Maharuq — the primordial whose rising pressure the Waking represents.
- Stormwatch Vault Opening — the event that marked its beginning.
- Vishara, the Captains — agents responding to the Waking, not causing it.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The cosmological mechanism is DM-layer; the downstream effects are player-observable.
