# The Hiding

**Canon File ID:** `forces/the-hiding`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/the-hiding`

**Entity Type:** Force — structural condition

**Retrieval Summary:** The Hiding is Dhurvaq's structural arrangement that separates Karsac from Exandrian awareness. Not a wall — a sustained condition that makes the attention required to act on knowledge of Karsac consistently redirect elsewhere. Halvash is the anomaly: the controlled point of contact that suggests either an intentional exception or a gap the Hiding cannot close. Serris is the closest any Cobalt Soul scholar has come to connecting the Hiding's fragments.

**Use For:** Identifying the Hiding as the force keeping Karsac unknown to Exandria, routing to Halvash's anomalous status.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/the-hiding`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Holding (Dhurvaq's protective micro-force keeping specific people specific; distinct), The Divine Gate (the structure added to at the Divergence; the Hiding's second layer was added then but the Gate is distinct).

**Key Relationships:**
- Dhurvaq — its author.
- Halvash — the anomaly in its structure.
- Serris — working to understand it.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. Full mechanism is DM-layer; Halvash's anomalous quality is player-observable.
