# The Divine Gate

**Canon File ID:** `forces/the-divine-gate`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/the-divine-gate`

**Entity Type:** Force — historical structure

**Retrieval Summary:** The Divine Gate was raised at the Divergence to seal the Betrayer Gods from the mortal world. In the noise of every boundary being redrawn, Dhurvaq added the Hiding's second layer — deepening Karsac's separation from Exandrian awareness. From the outside, this was an unintended consequence of the Gate's construction. From Dhurvaq's perspective, it was deliberate.

**Use For:** Identifying the Divine Gate as the historical structure and the Hiding's second-layer context.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/the-divine-gate`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Hiding (the ongoing condition; the Gate is the event that enabled its deepening, not the Hiding itself), The Divergence (the historical event at which the Gate was raised; distinct from the Gate as structure).

**Key Relationships:**
- The Divergence — the event at which it was raised.
- The Hiding — its construction allowed Dhurvaq to add the second layer.
- Dhurvaq — acted deliberately in the Gate's construction.
