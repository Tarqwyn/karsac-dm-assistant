# Yantravaq — The Collective

**Canon File ID:** `forces/yantravaq-collective`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/yantravaq-collective`

**Entity Type:** Force — collective / cosmological grouping

**Retrieval Summary:** The Yantravaq are three Instruments of Maharuq — Vishara (Principle of Dissipation), Qathar, and Sukaveth. They are not gods and not primordial forces. They are intelligences shaped by millennia of proximity to Maharuq into specific instruments that serve a nature which, paradoxically, abhors everything they are. They have existed since before Karsac was named. Vishara is the active one in Campaign 1. This file is the authoritative collective entry; the faction file at factions/yantravaq redirects here.

**Use For:** Identifying the Yantravaq as the three-Instrument collective, routing to individual Instrument files, and distinguishing the collective from Maharuq.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/yantravaq-collective`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Maharuq (the primordial force they serve; not the Yantravaq), Vishara alone (one of the three; the Campaign 1 active one), Dhurvaq (the opposing primordial; not one of the Yantravaq).

**Key Relationships:**
- Vishara — First Instrument; Principle of Dissipation; active in Campaign 1.
- Qathar — Second Instrument.
- Sukaveth — Third Instrument.
- Maharuq — the primordial condition that shaped them.
