# Dhurvaq

**Canon File ID:** `forces/dhurvaq`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/dhurvaq`

**Entity Type:** Force — primordial

**Retrieval Summary:** Dhurvaq is one of two primordial conditions that predate the gods — the principle of boundary and persistence, holding the condition that one thing is not another. It does not act by will; it acts by nature. Its instruments include the Hiding (the force that keeps Karsac unknown to Exandria) and the Holding (which keeps specific people specific where Vishara would smooth them). Brynja Thorgrimsdotter's resistance to Vishara's fog is the Holding's expression.

**Use For:** Identifying Dhurvaq as the primordial counter-force to Maharuq, routing to the Hiding and the Holding as its instruments, distinguishing it from Maharuq.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/dhurvaq`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Maharuq (the opposing primordial; dissolution vs boundary), The Hiding (Dhurvaq's instrument that keeps Karsac hidden; a distinct entity), The Holding (Dhurvaq's protective force that keeps specific people specific; distinct).

**Key Relationships:**
- Maharuq — the opposing primordial; Dhurvaq is the fixed star to Maharuq's dissolution.
- The Hiding — one of Dhurvaq's instruments.
- The Holding — another of Dhurvaq's instruments; responsible for Brynja's resistance.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. Full primordial cosmology is DM-layer.
