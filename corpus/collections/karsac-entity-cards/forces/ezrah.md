# Ezrah

**Canon File ID:** `forces/ezrah`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/forces/ezrah`

**Entity Type:** Force — god

**Retrieval Summary:** Ezrah is the god of Liberation and Terror — "the door that opens." Ezrah operates outside the canonical Exandrian pantheon and is possibly Dhurvaq's echo in the divine register. Erik Mathr serves Ezrah. The Cult of Ezrah's methodology — naming cages, opening conditions for liberation — is the divine-scale expression of Dhurvaq's boundary-and-persistence principle working against Vishara's dissolution.

**Use For:** Identifying Ezrah as the liberation deity and Erik Mathr's god, routing to the Paradox of Instruments.

**Primary Detail File:** `forces/ezrah`

**Do Not Confuse With:** Dhurvaq (the primordial whose echo Ezrah may be; distinct entity), Ioun (the god the Cobalt Soul serves; distinct deity entirely).

**Key Relationships:**
- Erik Mathr — his cleric; the resistance operative.
- Dhurvaq — possibly Ezrah's primordial echo.
- Cult of Ezrah — the resistance network.

**Visibility Note:** Mixed. The Dhurvaq connection is DM-layer.
