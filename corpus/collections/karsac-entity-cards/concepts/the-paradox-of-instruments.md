# The Paradox of the Instruments

**Canon File ID:** `concepts/the-paradox-of-instruments`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/concepts/the-paradox-of-instruments`

**Entity Type:** Concept — cosmological

**Retrieval Summary:** The Yantravaq (Vishara, Qathar, Sukaveth) use Dhurvaq's tools — specificity, identity, naming — to serve Maharuq's dissolution direction. Ezrah uses what appear to be Maharuq tools — liberation, the opening of doors, the naming of cages — to serve Dhurvaq's boundary-preservation. And a full waking of Maharuq would destroy the Yantravaq along with everything else.

**Use For:** Identifying the Paradox of Instruments as the core cosmological irony of the campaign, routing to Ezrah and the Yantravaq.

**Primary Detail File:** `concepts/the-paradox-of-instruments`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Thinning of the Particular (Vishara's operational method; distinct concept).

**Key Relationships:**
- Yantravaq — the instruments caught in the paradox.
- Ezrah — the god who works the other side of it.
- Maharuq — whose full waking ends the Yantravaq's existence too.

**Visibility Note:** DM-only.
