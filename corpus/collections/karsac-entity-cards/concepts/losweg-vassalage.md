# Lösweg Vassalage to Zörsdkog

**Canon File ID:** `concepts/losweg-vassalage`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/concepts/losweg-vassalage`

**Entity Type:** Concept — political relationship

**Retrieval Summary:** Lösweg is formally vassal to Zörsdkog — but the vassalage is of honour, not submission. Lösweg chose it: a people who honour their gods precisely because those gods chose to face an unwinnable war with dignity find in King Dugweb something they understand without translation. This is the political relationship that Mathr has been manipulating through managed annual visits for sixty years.

**Use For:** Identifying the Lösweg-Zörsdkog vassalage as the political context, routing to King Dugweb and the annual visit.

**Primary Detail File:** `concepts/losweg-vassalage`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Inner Council (the political mechanism; distinct from the concept of the vassalage itself).

**Key Relationships:**
- King Dugweb — the vassal-lord; the relationship is to him specifically.
- Jarl Mathr — has managed this relationship for sixty years, shaping what Dugweb sees.
