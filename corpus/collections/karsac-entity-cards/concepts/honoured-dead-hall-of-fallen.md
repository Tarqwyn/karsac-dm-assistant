# The Honoured Dead and the Hall of the Fallen

**Canon File ID:** `concepts/honoured-dead-hall-of-fallen`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/concepts/honoured-dead-hall-of-fallen`

**Entity Type:** Concept — Lösweg theology

**Retrieval Summary:** In Lösweg theology, those who die honourably are gathered to Vorath's Hall — where they feast and train between death and Ragnarholm, the foretold final battle. They wait. The Skalds' ability to speak with them is the living tradition's connection to the accumulated dead. When Vishara thins the Skalds, it severs the living from their honoured dead.

**Use For:** Identifying the Honoured Dead as the Lösweg afterlife theology and the cosmological stakes of the Skald tradition.
