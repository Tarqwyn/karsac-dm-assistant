# The Thinning of the Particular

**Canon File ID:** `concepts/the-thinning-of-the-particular`

**Entity Card ID:** `entity-cards/concepts/the-thinning-of-the-particular`

**Entity Type:** Concept

**Retrieval Summary:** The Thinning of the Particular is the term for Vishara's signature operation at community and cultural scale. Three named expressions: the Quieting (specific voices stop carrying), the Forgetting (specific memories stop being maintained), and the Smoothing (specific distinctions stop being noticed). Vishara does not destroy — it dilutes. The tree is still there. The edge is what goes. Törweg's half-empty harbour, the voices carrying too far, and the burned tapestries in Valweg are all expressions of this.

**Use For:** Identifying the Thinning as the conceptual framework for Vishara's community-scale operation, routing to the specific mechanisms and their effects in Törweg.

**Primary Detail File:** `concepts/the-thinning-of-the-particular`

**Do Not Confuse With:** The Threshold-Caught (a different concept; the individual-scale Vishara effect; not the community-scale Thinning), Vishara (the force; the Thinning is how it operates, not Vishara itself).

**Key Relationships:**
- Vishara — the force whose methodology the Thinning describes.
- Törweg — the primary visible expression of the Thinning in Chapter 2.

**Visibility Note:** Player-safe. Presented as observable phenomena the players have already seen.
