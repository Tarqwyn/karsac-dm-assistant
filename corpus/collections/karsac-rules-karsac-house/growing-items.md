# Growing Items

**Rule ID:** `rules/house/growing-items`

**Retrieval Summary:** Karsac growing items do not awaken automatically on level-up; level opens the door and story turns the key.

## Rule Summary

The Karsac growing-item model is story-triggered. Mechanical growth follows level bands, but the actual unlock comes from play.

## Procedure

1. Check whether the item has reached the relevant level band.
2. Check whether the story trigger for that state has happened.
3. Reveal perception, symbolism, and recognition before raw combat power where possible.
4. Keep early growth sideways rather than purely numeric.
5. Treat the full growth table in `mechanics/growing-items` as the detailed source for item-specific awakenings.

## At the Table

Use this when deciding whether a PC artefact has awakened, what form that awakening should take, and how much mechanical weight to reveal at once.

## Common Questions

### Does a level-up by itself awaken the item?

No. Level is a gate, not the trigger.

### What should an early awakening look like?

Usually narrative permission, recognition, sensing, warning, or a once-per-rest effect before large numerical boosts.
