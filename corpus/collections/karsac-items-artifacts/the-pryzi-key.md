# The Pryzi Key

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-pryzi-key`
**Retrieval Summary:** The key to the Pryzi Mountain vault; recovered by Shadow-Walkers from the Halvash archive in Session 1; currently in Yantravaq possession.

> The key to the Pryzi Mountain vault; stolen from the Cobalt Soul archive at Halvash in Session 1; in Yantravaq possession.

## Overview

The Pryzi key was held in the Cobalt Soul archive at Halvash. Three Shadow-Walkers came for it in Session 1. One died in the alley. Two escaped with the key aboard a Lösweg fishing trawler. It is currently in Yantravaq possession.

## DM-Only

Whether the players recover the key during Campaign 1 determines how much leverage they carry into Campaign 2. If they recover it: access to the Pryzi vault; the vault's contents become the Campaign 2 opening question. If they do not: the Yantravaq have it; the opening question is what was taken and what it means that the wrong hands have it. Either way the key is the thread forward.

The vault's contents are Campaign 2 scope — not specified in current sources.
