## Overview

Brynja has been keeping a private record for two years — dates, vessel descriptions, rough cargo weights. She shows it to the company in her hall, watching their faces for the moment they understand what she is describing. Some of them get there before she finishes. The rest get there at the warehouse.

On the happy path at session close, she gives them the record before they leave. Post as handout H5 when Brynja shares it.
