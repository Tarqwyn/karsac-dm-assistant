# The Pierced River-Stone

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-pierced-river-stone`
**Retrieval Summary:** The seventh object; the pierced river-stone; in Floki's coat; he has told no one; the most significant of the twelve binding components.

> The seventh object; Floki's coat; he has told no one.

## Overview

A stone that fits in the curve of a hand, pierced once through, smooth as river-rock. Floki set it apart from the other six objects in the cargo cabin at dawn. He did not say why. Sygna's eyes flicked to it once and then deliberately did not again. She has been told to make a list. She has been told not to ask.

The players did not see Floki set it apart. They saw six objects. There is a seventh.

## DM-Only

The pierced river-stone is the most significant component of the binding set — or the most significant single artefact among the twelve. Floki recognised it from the oldest layer of Karsac oral tradition the moment he held it. He felt, without being able to articulate it, that it should travel separately. He has told no one.

This is the major withheld secret of Chapter 2: not the Mathr genealogy, not the south dock token, but this. Floki is carrying something more significant than he knows how to say, and he has decided not to say it until he understands it better. His grandmother's second half of the prophecy is connected to this object.

Per `meta/dm-secret-policy`: the pierced river-stone's significance is never disclosed in player-facing retrieval. Never hint at its existence.
