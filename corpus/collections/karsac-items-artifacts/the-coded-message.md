# The Coded Message

**Canon File ID:** `items/the-coded-message`
**Retrieval Summary:** A coded message carried by Erwing from a dying Cobalt monk to Serris; decoded text names 'the Cleric' and warns they are discovered; Serris does not know who the Cleric is.

> Carried by Erwing; delivered to Serris; decoded: "Get word to the Cleric. I am discovered, trust no one, continue the work."

## Overview

The coded message was given to Erwing Smallfoot by a Cobalt monk on the road to Bysaes Tyl — a monk who had been discovered by Mathr's network. The monk asked Erwing to find Cumbria, then find Serris, and deliver it. Erwing does not know what it says. He does not know why it matters.

Serris reads it in Brynja's hall that evening and goes very quiet.

## Decoded Content

*"Get word to the Cleric. I am discovered, trust no one, continue the work. It is now more important than ever. They are close to discovery. We must not allow them to succeed."*

Serris does not know who the Cleric is. The players may piece it together.

## DM-Only

"The Cleric" is Erik Mathr — the Ezrah cleric working inside Vishara's political cage from within. The Cobalt monk who gave Erwing the message was part of Erik's network, now discovered. The message's delivery is the first indication to Serris that the network she has been adjacent to is in danger. It sets the chain for Sessions 3–4: get word to the Cleric, which means finding Erik.
