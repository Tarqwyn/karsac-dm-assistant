# Folded Name — Mathr

**Canon File ID:** `items/folded-name-mathr`

> Brynja writes a name; tells them not to open it until they are on the Greyback. Handout H17.

## Overview

At the session's close, Brynja writes Mathr's name on a piece of paper, folds it, and tells the company not to open it until they are on the Greyback. She will not say the name aloud in her hall. She has learned that saying certain names aloud in Törweg does not feel safe. The Truthspeaker told her not to speak to anyone about this. She is writing, not speaking.

Unopened at the session's end. Contents confirmed DM-only: the name is Jarl Mathr. The paper is the first time the players have the name given directly — not inferred from the token or the records, but handed.

Post as handout H17 as Brynja hands it over.
