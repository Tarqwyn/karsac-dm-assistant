# Mathr Family Archive Page

**Canon File ID:** `items/mathr-family-archive-page`
**Retrieval Summary:** Council archive page documenting the Mathr and Vane genealogies; the Mathr entry has no antecedent before 1263; the Vane entry is traceable to Havnarvik; handout H_REC.

> Council archive genealogy page; Mathr family appears fully formed in 1263 with no antecedent; Vane family traceable to Havnarvik. Handout H_REC.

## Overview

The council archive page documenting the Mathr and Vane genealogies. Two columns: Mathr's record, beginning abruptly in 1263 with no oral-historical antecedent; Vane's record, properly traced back through five generations to a fishing village in Havnarvik. A marginal note in initials H-D traces the Vane lineage to Havnarvik specifically.

Post as handout H_REC when a PC investigates the council archives in Thread D.

## DM-Only

The absence of Mathr antecedent is structural — not a missing record, but the record system itself not containing the entry that should precede 1263. The oral tradition has no memory of House Mathr before it appeared. For a tradition as complete as Lösweg's genealogical record, this is not an oversight. It is an impossibility.
