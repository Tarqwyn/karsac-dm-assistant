# The Cobalt Soul

**Canon File ID:** `factions/cobalt-soul`

> Order within the church of Ioun; dedicated to the preservation and distribution of truth; archive at Halvash; the closest any organisation has come to connecting the Hiding's fragments.

## Overview

The Cobalt Soul is an order within the church of Ioun, the Knowing Mistress, dedicated to the preservation and distribution of truth. It maintains an archive at Halvash — the anomalous port that is Karsac's controlled point of contact with Exandria — and in so doing has accumulated, over time, more fragments of Karsac knowledge than any other Exandrian institution.

## Description

### Mission

The Cobalt Soul's mission is epistemic: find, preserve, and distribute truth. In Halvash, this mandate runs directly into the Hiding's mechanisms — the absence of supporting structure that prevents knowledge of Karsac from propagating. The archive holds records that do not connect to each other because the connections require knowledge no Exandrian scholar has been prepared to receive.

Serris has been building that preparatory knowledge piece by piece for three years. She is the closest any Cobalt Soul scholar has come to connecting the fragments.

### The Pryzi Key

The Shadow-Walkers came to the Halvash archive to extract the Pryzi key, which the Cobalt Soul held (apparently without knowing what it was, or why). The Cobalt Soul's seal on the archive room was broken during the Session 1 alley fight. Two Shadow-Walkers escaped with the key. One died. See `events/halvash-alley-fight`.

### The Coded Message

Erwing Smallfoot was tasked with delivering a coded message to Serris from a Cobalt monk who had been discovered. The message: *"Get word to the Cleric. I am discovered, trust no one, continue the work."* Serris does not yet know who the Cleric is. See `items/the-coded-message` and `npcs/erwing`.
