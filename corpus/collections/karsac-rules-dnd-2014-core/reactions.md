# Reactions

**Rule ID:** `rules/core/reactions`

**Retrieval Summary:** A reaction is a special response to a trigger, and you get only one until the start of your next turn.

## Rule Summary

Reactions happen in response to triggers on your turn or someone else's turn.

## Procedure

1. Wait for a trigger created by a rule, spell, feature, or situation.
2. If you have a reaction available, choose whether to use it.
3. Resolve the reaction immediately.
4. You cannot take another reaction until the start of your next turn.

## At the Table

Use this for opportunity attacks, reaction spells, readied actions, and defensive features.

## Common Questions

### Can I use more than one reaction in a round?

No. Once you use one, you wait until the start of your next turn.

### Does a reaction stop the current turn?

It interrupts the turn, then that creature continues right after the reaction resolves.
