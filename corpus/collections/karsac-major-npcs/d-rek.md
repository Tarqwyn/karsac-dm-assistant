> Innkeeper of the Meridian, Halvash; twenty years; knows the company is not done with the night.

## Overview

D'rek has kept the Meridian inn in Halvash for twenty years. She knows — the way an innkeeper of twenty years knows — when people are not done with the night. After the alley fight, the company returned. She poured drinks. She put bread and salt on the table. She put one extra chair near the hearth. She did not ask what happened.

She is a threshold-keeper in the small sense: the keeper of the space between the event and the next thing, who understands that the most useful thing she can do is be present without demand.
