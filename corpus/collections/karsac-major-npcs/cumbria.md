# Cumbria

> Halvashi pilot aboard the Greyback; speaks almost no Common; navigates by things the players cannot perceive.

## Overview

Cumbria is the Halvashi pilot who navigates the Greyback across the Whale-Road from Halvash to Törweg. The crossing takes two days. She sails at first light and does not wait. She communicates through adjustments to the tiller, occasional gestures, and the particular quality of her attention to the water.

She reads the colour of the water as though it were script. Twice on the first morning she adjusts course for reasons nobody aboard can see, and everyone aboard accepts without comment. When the water darkens to wet-slate and the depth seems greater than the sounding line suggests, she steers through it more slowly than she has been steering. Nobody speaks while they are in it. When they are through, she exhales.

She is a practical presence — the Whale-Road's navigator, not a narrative figure. Her function is to deliver the company and then leave.
