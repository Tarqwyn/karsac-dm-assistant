# Serris

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/serris`
**Retrieval Summary:** Cobalt Soul archivist at Halvash; closest any scholar has come to connecting the Hiding's fragments; asked to travel with the company after three years of working up to it.

> Cobalt Soul archivist at Halvash; closest any scholar has come to connecting the Hiding's fragments; has been working up to asking for three years.

## Overview

Serris is a Cobalt Soul archivist at the Halvash archive. They asked to travel with the company. They had been working up to asking for three years. The company said yes.

The Sourcebook's Appendix A notes: Serris is the closest any Cobalt Soul scholar has come to connecting the fragments the Hiding has left in Exandrian consciousness — the disconnected records, the charts that don't connect, the knowledge that fails to propagate because the supporting structure isn't there. They have been building that supporting structure piece by piece.

## Description

Described consistently in sources as methodical, observant, and specifically excited by pattern recognition. On the Greyback, they notice the geometry of the padding impressions around the cargo before anyone else and say nothing yet, but the geometry is bothering them. In the Maw, they recognise the connection between the Aeorian note's handwriting and the scroll case's margin — *"the same hand. After two thousand years"* — and go very quiet.

They do not go quiet because they are frightened. They go quiet because the thing they have been building toward for three years has just connected.

## Voice & Bearing

Careful, precise, quietly excited when they find what they are looking for. They tend to make notes, physical ones, in a small book. They ask questions about evidence before they ask questions about meaning. They arrived at the Halvash archive from Vushhiri training and have spent years applying Vushhiri epistemic discipline to material that Vushhiri training was not designed to handle.

## Motivations

**What they want:** to connect the fragments. To understand what the Hiding is. To not be the person who was closest and missed it.

**What they carry:** the coded message, after Erwing delivers it. They read it in Brynja's hall and go very quiet — a different quiet than the Maw quiet. They do not know who the Cleric is.
