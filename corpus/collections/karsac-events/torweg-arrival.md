# The Törweg Arrival

**Canon File ID:** `events/torweg-arrival`
**Retrieval Summary:** The Greyback arrives at dawn; three figures on the wharf; the flag at half-mast; Brynja waiting; the man with the black pin; Ragnfrid's three private observations.

> Dawn of day three; the Stormwatch Mountains out of the dark; three figures on the wharf; the flag at half-mast; nobody knows for whom.

## Overview

Dawn of day three. The Stormwatch Mountains emerging from pre-dawn darkness, dark teeth against a sky going slowly silver. Törweg sits in a deep bay — timber-and-stone buildings climbing the western cliff face, a single long stone wharf, longships on the gravelled beach. The bay should be full of fishing vessels. It is not.

Three figures on the wharf. The flag at the harbourmaster's tower at half-mast. Voices carrying clean and a little too far — reaching the deck before the ship is alongside. The voices stop as the Greyback comes in.

Brynja stands waiting. The man with the black pin stands at the far end.

*"Floki. You came back with strangers. Why."*

## Ragnfrid's Three Observations

Privately delivered to Ragnfrid's player as Törweg resolves from the mist:

1. The two men flanking Brynja are not Floki's clan-tied kin. They are armed. This welcome does not include them.
2. The flag flies half only for council or close-kin dead. Somebody who registers has died.
3. Brynja's address to Floki is in council cadence — the Skald register. She is being watched. She is telling him she is being watched.
