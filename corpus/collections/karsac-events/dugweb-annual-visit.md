# King Dugweb's Annual Visit

**Canon File ID:** `events/dugweb-annual-visit`
**Retrieval Summary:** King Dugweb's approaching annual visit to Lösweg; Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years; Qathar has been preparing a threshold around this one; approaching in seven weeks.

> Approaching in seven weeks; Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years; Qathar has prepared a threshold; the players' route races to get there first.

## Overview

King Dugweb visits Lösweg once a year. Jarl Mathr has managed every visit for sixty years — keeping the council, keeping the city, keeping a carefully constructed silence between Dugweb and the country he has come to see.

The visit is approaching in seven weeks from the Chapter 2 events. Brynja's closing recommendation: if the players can reach Valweg before Dugweb's visit and put the truth in front of Jarl Beorn, they may have the only chance anyone has had to show Dugweb what Mathr has kept him from seeing.

The road north is not safe. Brynja says so. Floki says so.

## DM-Only

Qathar has been arranging something around this visit for sixty years. Whatever it has arranged will be expressing by the time the players reach Valweg. The annual visit is the threshold Qathar has prepared. Whichever way it falls, Qathar has worked to ensure the fall is permanent. See `forces/qathar`.
