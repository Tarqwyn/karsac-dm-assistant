# Session 1 — Salsvault Discovery

**Canon File ID:** `events/session-1-salsvault`
**Retrieval Summary:** Session 1 (or pre-campaign); the company entered Salsvault for an antidote; destroyed the Frigid Woe phials; found the scroll case priced at fifteen gold among ordinary stock.

> Someone was sick; the antidote was in Salsvault; the scroll case was there priced at fifteen gold.

## Overview

The company entered Salsvault, an Aeorian pre-Calamity bioweapons laboratory under the ice of Eiselcross, because someone was sick and the antidote was there. They destroyed the Frigid Woe phials. They left with the scroll case — priced at fifteen gold, stored as ordinary inventory.

No intelligence arranged this. No plan produced it. It is the purest accident in a chain of events otherwise shaped at every point by someone's intention.

*They are carrying the answer since Session 1. They do not know this until Session 6.*
