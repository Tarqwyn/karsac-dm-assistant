# The Maw Investigation

**Canon File ID:** `events/maw-investigation`
**Retrieval Summary:** Thread B; two hours into the Stormwatch foothills; the Aeorian note missed by Vane's people; the wall geometry continues the bone disc; the Threshold-Bound Echo.

> Thread B; two hours up; three discoveries; the Threshold-Bound Echo; the same hand as the scroll case.

## Overview

Thread B in Scene 4. Two hours on the foothill path, east of Törweg. The birds stop about twenty minutes before the Maw appears. The fracture — eight feet wide at its widest, narrowing to a slot — scores the rock with the bone disc pattern continued.

Three discoveries: the iron sconce left by Vane's people (they were here); the Aeorian note missed by Vane's housecarls (same hand as the scroll case margin — Serris goes quiet); the Threshold-Bound Echo in the lower shaft.

The encounter with the Echo is the Thread B combat. Tester, not finisher. Three rounds, then it withdraws — unless the players fight badly.
