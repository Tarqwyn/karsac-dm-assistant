## Overview

Thread D in Scene 4. Players investigating the council archives find the genealogical records that prove House Mathr's structural impossibility.

Serris names it: *"Mathr's family has no antecedent. Vane's family has antecedent, but their elevation is contemporaneous with Mathr's family's appearance. The Vanes are real. Mathr's family are not. This is not how Lösweg families work."*

The arithmetic implication: Mathr appears in records sixty years ago as a man in his forties and has not appeared to age. Vane has never done this arithmetic. Players may.
