# Brynja's Briefing

**Canon File ID:** `events/brynjas-briefing`
**Retrieval Summary:** Brynja takes the company to her hall; shows her records; names four threads (Mode A) or opens the city (Mode B); the first substantive intelligence transfer.

> Brynja's hall; records shown; four threads named or city opened; the first substantive intelligence transfer.

## Overview

Scene 4's opening: Brynja takes the company to her hall, offers food, and tells them what she has been keeping records of — watching their faces for the moment they understand what she is describing. Then she gives them their direction.

**Mode A (structured spine):** Brynja explicitly names four threads — Brix at the Saltbone; the Maw in the foothills; Pell Duvash in the upper market; the council archives. Players split, investigate, reconvene.

**Mode B (open world):** Brynja says: *"Walk the city. Listen. Come back to me when you have heard something."* Players discover what they discover. Mode B is available at player request; Mode A is the default, especially for online play.

Tailored per-PC hooks are delivered as the company makes ready to leave the hall — Brynja reads each PC in two sentences and points them at their thread.
