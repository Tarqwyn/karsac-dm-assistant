# Kohlmar

**Canon File ID:** `npcs/kohlmar`
**Retrieval Summary:** Bruithwr captain who collected the final artefact consignment from Pell Duvash; the south-bound chain's endpoint; named by Vane and by Duvash.

> Bruithwr captain; collected artefacts from Duvash on Vane's orders; the south-bound chain's named endpoint.

## Overview

Kohlmar is the captain out of Bruithwr who collected artefacts from Pell Duvash as the final link in the south-bound chain. He is named by both Vane (as the recipient) and Duvash (as the captain who collected the last consignment). He is at the wheel of the coastal vessel moored at the south dock when the Chapter 2 confrontation takes place.

He is not a significant figure in the current session — his significance is as a thread forward: if the players recover `items/aldric-letter`, the letter names him as the Bruithwr contact. The south-bound chain and what awaits in Bruithwr are Session 3 onwards material.
