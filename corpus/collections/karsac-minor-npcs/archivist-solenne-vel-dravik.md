**Canon File ID:** `npcs/archivist-solenne-vel-dravik`

> Vushhiri archivist; quoted witnessing a Lösweg burial at sea; Lösweg scholarly authority.

Archivist Solenne vel Dravik is quoted in the Sourcebook's Chapter 2 epigraph: *"They do not fear the sea because they were born from it. They do not fear death because their gods died first — and rose again"* — written upon first witnessing a Lösweg burial at sea. She represents the Vushhiri faculty's most direct experiential authority on Lösweg culture and its relationship to death. No further characterisation beyond this epigraph.
