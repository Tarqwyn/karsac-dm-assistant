# Chapter Outline: The Salt and Shadow of Fiska

## Chapter Purpose
This chapter shifts the party's focus eastward, introducing a new location and deepening the mystery surrounding the Pryzi key and the wider machinations of Vishara. It will provide opportunities for investigation, social interaction, and potential combat, while also subtly advancing several simmering threads. The chapter aims to reveal more about the stolen key's purpose and connect it to a larger network of influence.

## Starting State
The party is currently in Karsac, following the trail of the Pryzi key and the Shadow-Walkers who stole it. They possess the original bone disc, which Korvann is eager to examine. They are aware of the coded message intended for Serris and the potential threat posed by Vishara’s influence.

## Player Knowledge
KnownFacts is 0. The party is aware of the hot threads listed in the campaign state, but has no confirmed knowledge beyond what has been directly observed or explicitly stated.

## DM Truth
*   **Pell Duvash's Connection:** Pell Duvash, the merchant in Torweg, is not merely a conduit for old objects. He is a former agent of the Cobalt Monks, tasked with gathering information on Vishara's activities. He was forced into hiding after being betrayed.
*   **The Pryzi Key's Purpose:** The Pryzi key doesn’t unlock a vault filled with treasure. It unlocks a hidden archive within the Fiska docks – a repository of forbidden knowledge related to the ancient Khôràna civilization and a potential source of power Vishara seeks to exploit.
*   **Mathr’s Involvement:** Jarl Mathr is subtly manipulating events in Fiska, using the town's trade routes to funnel resources and information to Vishara. He believes he is securing Karsac’s future, but is being used as a pawn.
*   **Rowan's Ancestor:** Rowahn, Rowan's ancestor, was a Khôràna scholar who fled to Karsac with a vital piece of the archive's knowledge. His descendants have unknowingly carried a latent connection to the archive's power.

## Core Pressure
The party must locate the Shadow-Walkers and recover the Pryzi key, while simultaneously uncovering the truth behind the stolen goods and the growing influence of Vishara. The pressure is amplified by the knowledge that someone is actively manipulating events in Karsac, and the party is likely being watched.

## Active Factions and NPCs
*   **Jarl Mathr:** Wants to secure Karsac’s future, believes he’s acting in its best interest. Knows nothing of Vishara’s direct involvement. Will attempt to subtly steer the party away from Fiska if they start asking uncomfortable questions.
*   **Vishara:** Wants to acquire the knowledge contained within the Pryzi archive. Knows the party possesses the bone disc and is investigating. Will use agents and subtle influence to obstruct their progress.
*   **Pell Duvash:** Wants to expose Vishara’s network. Knows he is being hunted. Will cautiously offer information to those he deems trustworthy.
*   **Captain Cumbria:** Wants to return to Halvash. Knows nothing of the Pryzi key or Vishara. Will be a potential source of information about travel and local contacts.
*   **The Shadow-Walkers:** (Unknown motivations) Currently acting as agents of Vishara. Their loyalty is questionable.

## Scene Spine
1.  **Purpose:** Arrival and Initial Investigation. **Location:** Fiska docks. **Pressure:** Time is of the essence. **Choices:** Question locals, investigate the docks, seek out information brokers. **Clues:** A nervous dockworker mentions seeing the Shadow-Walkers. **Failure:** Immediate suspicion from the local guard, hindering further investigation.
2.  **Purpose:** Meeting Pell Duvash. **Location:** A hidden tavern in Fiska’s upper market. **Pressure:** Duvash is paranoid and reluctant to trust. **Choices:** Persuasion, intimidation, offering assistance. **Clues:** Duvash reveals he knows more than he lets on about the stolen goods. **Failure:** Duvash refuses to speak, forcing the party to seek information elsewhere.
3.  **Purpose:** Infiltration of the Docks Archive. **Location:** Hidden archive beneath the Fiska docks. **Pressure:** Security measures, potential traps. **Choices:** Stealth, brute force, social engineering. **Clues:** Fragments of Khôràna text, a map hinting at a larger network. **Failure:** Triggering an alarm, alerting the local guard and potentially Vishara’s agents.
4.  **Purpose:** Confrontation with a Shadow-Walker. **Location:** The docks or a nearby warehouse. **Pressure:** Combat encounter, potential for escape. **Choices:** Negotiation, combat, pursuit. **Clues:** The Shadow-Walker possesses a coded message related to Mathr. **Failure:** The Shadow-Walker escapes, taking valuable information with them.
5.  **Purpose:** Uncovering Mathr’s Involvement. **Location:** Jarl Mathr’s estate in Fiska. **Pressure:** Social encounter, potential for exposure. **Choices:** Diplomacy, accusation, investigation. **Clues:** Evidence of Mathr’s communication with an unknown party. **Failure:** Mathr denies any wrongdoing, further obfuscating the truth.

## Optional Scenes
*   **The Crow's Guidance:** Rowan experiences a vision or encounter with the Crow, offering cryptic guidance related to Fiska's history.
*   **The Merchant's Plea:** A desperate merchant seeks the party’s help in recovering stolen goods, providing a minor side quest.
*   **The Local Dispute:** A brewing conflict between two Fiska families provides a distraction or opportunity for gathering information.

## Clues and Reveals
*   **Player-Safe Clues:** Nervous dockworker sightings, the existence of a hidden archive, Pell Duvash’s reluctance to speak.
*   **Deeper Implications:** The Pryzi key unlocks forbidden knowledge, Mathr is being manipulated, Rowan’s ancestor played a crucial role in Khôràna history.
*   **DM-Only Truth:** The archive contains a ritual to amplify latent magical abilities, which Vishara intends to use.

## Adversaries and Obstacles
*   **Social:** Jarl Mathr’s influence, Pell Duvash’s paranoia, local guards.
*   **Procedural:** Navigating Fiska’s social hierarchy, deciphering coded messages, bypassing security measures.
*   **Factional:** Shadow-Walkers, Vishara’s agents. (Refer to existing Vishara adversary corpus).
*   **Investigative:** Uncovering Mathr’s involvement, identifying the source of the stolen goods.
*   **Combat:** Shadow-Walkers, potentially hired mercenaries.

## Fail-Forward Paths
*   **Missing Clues:** The party overlooks a crucial detail, allowing the Shadow-Walkers to escape with vital information.
*   **Delaying the Hook:** The party gets sidetracked by a minor conflict, giving Vishara’s agents time to consolidate their position.
*   **Refusing the Hook:** The party dismisses Pell Duvash’s warnings, leading them to underestimate the danger they face.

## End Conditions
*   **Success:** The party recovers the Pryzi key, uncovers Mathr’s involvement, and gains a deeper understanding of Vishara’s plan.
*   **Partial:** The party recovers the Pryzi key but fails to expose Mathr’s involvement, leaving a lingering threat.
*   **Failure:** The party fails to recover the Pryzi key, allowing Vishara to gain access to the archive and advance her agenda.
